## Understanding the Anime Story 2 Meta

The current meta in Anime Story 2 revolves around efficiency. Early on, your primary goal is to clear story stages as quickly as possible to unlock better farming spots. This means that units with "Full AoE" capabilities are valued much higher than single-target nukers. As you reach the endgame, the focus shifts toward status ailments like poison and marks, which amplify the damage your entire team deals to high-HP bosses.

According to veteran players, the distinction between a "good" unit and a "meta" unit often comes down to their traits and shiny status. A shiny unit provides a significant 25% stat buff, which can be the difference between one-shotting a wave or getting overwhelmed. When consulting an **anime story 2 tier list**, always consider that a lower-tier unit with a "Monarch" trait might occasionally outperform an S-tier unit with a basic trait in specific scenarios.

## Traits and Rerolls: Maximizing Your Potential

In Anime Story 2, a unit is only as good as its trait. Even an S-Tier unit can feel underwhelming if it has a common trait that doesn't synergize with its kit.

*   **Monarch:** The gold standard. This trait provides massive boosts to all stats and is the ultimate goal for any Secret unit like Remaru or Ten.
*   **Ethereal/Solar:** These are excellent high-tier traits that focus on damage and speed. They are perfect for units like Beiru who need to apply status effects quickly.
*   **Berserk:** A great early-to-mid-game trait. It provides a straightforward damage boost that helps units like Ace clear waves faster.

According to player feedback, you should never settle for a "Dead Eye" trait unless you have specifically built your gear around crit chance. Instead, save your rerolls for banners that offer increased chances for rainbow-tier traits.
