### S-Tier: Game Changers

These units are absolute must-haves, capable of carrying teams through the toughest content and accelerating your progress significantly.

*   **Tenin:** Widely considered one of the best for farming. While some players might argue for Beiru early on due to his mark and poison, Tenin's raw farming efficiency is unparalleled, making him a top priority for accumulating resources. *   **Devil Cyborg:** A phenomenal unit, especially for specific farming strategies. Community reports show that Devil Cyborg can be exploited in certain maps (like Hero City's double Devil Cyborg stage) where his attacks cause chain reactions, leading to incredibly fast clear times and bountiful rewards. His power makes him a prime candidate for raids and late-game challenges, solidifying his spot as a top-tier Secret.

### A-Tier: Elite Performers

These units are incredibly strong and will significantly bolster any team, providing excellent utility and damage. While not as universally game-breaking as S-tier, they are still highly desirable.

*   **Beiru:** A strong contender, especially in later stages. His ability to apply marks and poison makes him incredibly useful for increasing overall team damage, particularly against high-HP targets. While Tenin might be preferred for pure speed farming, Beiru's utility in more challenging content is undeniable.
*   **Gojo:** A reliable damage-over-time (DOT) unit that can contribute significantly to sustained damage output. Gojo is a solid pick for many teams, especially when paired with units that can hold enemies in place or benefit from prolonged engagements.
*   **Broly:** An excellent Mythic unit that, when upgraded to Secret or higher, can become a powerhouse. Broly's raw damage and AoE capabilities make him a strong choice for clearing waves and dealing with bosses, particularly useful in events like the Slime event.

### B-Tier: Solid Contributors

These units are good and can definitely find a place in your team, but they might be more situational or require specific team compositions to truly shine.

*   **Sid:** A very useful unit for specific farming methods, particularly the "SID technique" in Hero City. While his general combat prowess might not match S or A-tier, his ability to farm gems and gold overnight makes him incredibly valuable for resource accumulation. However, his effectiveness often relies on exploiting certain map mechanics.
*   **Ant King:** A decent Secret unit that can provide solid damage. While not as flashy as others, a well-built Ant King can still be a valuable asset, especially if you pull him early on and lack higher-tier options.

## Strategies for Acquiring Secret Units

Acquiring Secret units in *Anime Story 2* is often a game of patience and strategic resource management. Here are some tips:

*   **Save Your Gems:** Resist the urge to summon on every banner. Identify the Secret units you need most from our **Anime Story 2 secret tier list** and save your gems for banners that feature them with increased rates or as pity rewards.
*   **Utilize Codes:** Always redeem available codes for free gems, rerolls, and other valuable resources. These can provide a significant boost, especially for new players.
*   **AFK Farming:** As demonstrated by many players, AFK farming certain stages (like the Devil Cyborg bug in Hero City or the desert map for gems) can yield massive amounts of currency. This allows you to accumulate enough gems for pity summons when your desired Secret unit is on banner.
*   **Pity System:** Understand the pity system for Secret units. This guarantees you a Secret unit after a certain number of summons, making saving up gems even more critical.
*   **Event Participation:** Keep an eye on in-game events, as some may offer opportunities to acquire Secret units or their fragments.

## Maximizing Your Secret Units: Traits and Builds

Once you've acquired a Secret unit from the **Anime Story 2 secret tier list**, optimizing them through traits and leveling is the next step.

*   **Traits:** Aim for rainbow traits like Berserk, Solar, or Ethereal, as these provide significant boosts to damage or utility. For specific roles, a trait like Dead Eye can be powerful if you have the crit gear to support it. Rolling traits can be expensive, so prioritize your most important units.
*   **Leveling:** Leveling your units to their maximum (e.g., level 300) is crucial for unlocking their full potential. This requires a substantial amount of gold and experience.
*   **Ascension:** For Legendary units that can be ascended, collecting duplicates is key. While Secret units typically don't ascend in the same way, duplicates can sometimes be used for specific power-ups or to transfer shiny status.
*   **Shiny Units:** Shiny versions of units provide a 25% buff, making them highly desirable. If you manage to get a shiny version of a top-tier Secret, it's worth the investment to transfer its shiny status and maximize its potential. The cost of shiny transfer (e.g., 50 million gold) can seem steep, but gold becomes easier to acquire later in the game.
