## Essential Early Game Units: Your Anime Story 2 Beginner Tier List Picks

When you're just starting in *Anime Story 2*, focusing on units that offer high impact for relatively low investment is key. These characters will form the backbone of your early-game success, allowing you to clear content efficiently and gather resources for future upgrades.

### Mythic Powerhouses for New Players

Mythic units are often the most sought-after due to their raw power and unique abilities. While they can be harder to obtain, a select few stand out as exceptional choices for beginners.

*   **Broly (Mythic DPS):** Broly is an absolute beast for early-game damage. His high output makes him invaluable for clearing stages and even tackling events like the Rimuru event. If you pull Broly, consider yourself lucky and invest in him immediately. His damage potential helps you push through content much faster than other units.
*   **Genos (Secret Evolution DPS):** Although initially a secret unit, Genos has an evolution that transforms him into a secret-tier character. What makes him perfect for beginners is his relatively cheap evolution cost early on. He provides immense damage and is a fantastic long-term investment that pays off quickly. Look to acquire and evolve Genos as soon as possible.

### Legendary Gems: Underrated Picks

Legendary units, while not as rare as Mythics or Secrets, can still offer significant value, especially if you're struggling to acquire the top-tier rarities.

*   **Ace (Legendary DPS):** Ace is often an underrated pick for new players. As a Legendary, he's more accessible than Mythics, making him a viable option for early team composition. While ascending him will require more dupes due to his legendary status, his reliable damage output can carry you through many early stages.

Remember, the goal is to get strong units that can clear content. While a full S-tier team is ideal, building a solid foundation with accessible yet powerful units from this **Anime Story 2 beginner tier list** is your best bet.

## Understanding Unit Progression & Power-Ups

Acquiring strong units is only half the battle; knowing how to enhance them is equally critical. *Anime Story 2* offers several avenues for unit progression:

*   **Stats:** Units have various stats that can be improved using Stat Chips. There are normal Stat Chips and "Reroll All" Stat Chips. "Worthiness" is a crucial stat that increases the chance of getting higher or more powerful stats during rerolls.
*   **Shiny Variants:** Shiny units deal more damage than their normal counterparts. If you acquire a shiny version of a strong unit, prioritize it! You can transfer shinies to other units using Shiny Transfer.
*   **Traits:** Traits provide passive bonuses to your units. Aim for powerful traits like "Ethereal" or "Monarch" on your main damage dealers and support units. Traits are vital for optimizing your team's performance.
*   **Ascension:** Ascension increases a unit's maximum level cap. For example, sacrificing another copy of Goku to your main Goku can increase his level cap from 50 to 100, and up to 300 at Tier 5. Secret and ascended units typically start at level 300 without needing ascension.
*   **Evolution:** Some units, like Aizen and Escanor, have evolutions that significantly boost their power. These often require specific items or "stars" obtained from challenges.
*   **Relics:** Relics provide additional buffs. For DPS units, the "Baseball Bat" relic is highly recommended as it grants +50% damage. Other relics might boost HP, like the Elixir. Green shards for crafting relics are dropped from boss stages in each world.

## Farming Resources: Gems, Gold, & Rerolls

To continuously improve your units and summon new ones, you'll need a steady supply of resources.

*   **Gems:** Gems are primarily obtained through completing achievements and daily challenges. Defeating every stage on Normal, Hard, and Nightmare difficulties across all maps will yield a significant amount of gems. You also get gems for discovering new units in your index and from level milestones.
*   **Gold (Coins):** The most efficient way to farm gold is by repeatedly clearing the last boss stage of the latest world you can comfortably beat. This also drops brown shards, which are essential for crafting the best DPS relic, the Baseball Bat. The Coin Shop, which resets daily, also allows you to purchase various items, including rerolls and stars.
*   **Rerolls (Stat Chips):** Rerolls are crucial for optimizing your units' stats.
    *   **Tower Mode:** The Tower is an excellent source of rerolls. It offers infinite floors, and every 100 floors guarantees a Sid drop (a unit that can also be shiny). The Tower is considered one of the best ways to farm rerolls.
    *   **Challenges:** Daily and normal challenges (resetting every half hour) drop various currencies, including reroll stat chips and stars. Be aware that challenges often come with debuffs, like enemies having double stats.
    *   **Infinite Mode:** While effective in *Anime Story 1*, Infinite Mode is still a good source for rerolls and units in *Anime Story 2*. Finding a team composition that can efficiently clear a certain wave is key.
    *   **Slime Apocalypse Event:** If active, this event offers Slime Tokens, which can be exchanged for rerolls and other valuable items at the event shop.
    *   **Coin Shop:** The daily-resetting Coin Shop often sells reroll chips. Make sure to check it daily and purchase them if you have spare coins.

## Game Modes to Master

*Anime Story 2* features several game modes beyond the standard stage progression, each offering unique rewards and challenges.

*   **Raids:** Raids are crucial for acquiring powerful units and specific items.
    *   **Bleach Raids (Act 5 & 6):** These raids offer units like Aizen, who is considered a meta unit. Act 6 specifically drops shiny variants, which deal more damage. The raid shop allows you to buy luck potions, traits, and evolution items with "Hollow Tokens."
    *   **Naruto Raids (Act 5 & 6):** These raids feature units like Itachi and Pain. Their respective raid shop, "Ember Village," offers coin potions, stat chips, and rerolls using "Ninja Tokens."
    *   **Strategy:** Raids are generally mid-game content, requiring a semi-decent team to clear efficiently. Focus on farming shiny variants of units like Aizen to boost your overall damage.
*   **Challenges:** As mentioned, challenges are a prime source for stars (needed for evolutions like Pride's) and various currencies. They reset frequently and come with debuffs, so prepare your team accordingly.
*   **Tower:** The Tower is an infinite-floor game mode. Every 100 floors guarantees a Sid drop. It's an excellent place to farm rerolls and is often used to test optimal team compositions. The best teams for Tower usually include at least two support units and a tank like Pride.
*   **Slime Apocalypse Event:** This event, often available during special periods, involves defeating three stages of bosses, culminating in a powerful Rimuru. Your units' health does not reset between stages. Defeating Rimuru yields Slime Tokens, which can be used to summon event-exclusive units like Rimuru herself, or exchange for stat cubes, secret/mythic orbs, and various other currencies. Broly is particularly effective in this event due to his high damage output.
*   **Battle Pass:** The Battle Pass offers rewards as you progress through tiers. The free track provides useful items, while the paid track often includes unique units or shiny versions of battle pass units. While progression can be slow, *Anime Story 2* is an AFK game, so passive accumulation is expected.

## Optimizing Your Team Composition

A well-balanced team is essential for tackling various challenges in *Anime Story 2*. Generally, a strong team will consist of:

*   **Main DPS Units:** These are your primary damage dealers. Units like Broly, Genos, and Aizen (from raids) are excellent choices.
*   **Support Units:** Characters like Gojo and Rimuru (if you acquire her) provide crucial buffs and healing, significantly enhancing your team's survivability and damage output.
*   **Tank:** A dedicated tank, such as Pride, can absorb enemy damage, protecting your DPS and support units and allowing them to operate effectively.

Aim for a composition that includes at least two support units and one tank alongside your main damage dealers. This balanced approach will allow you to clear most content, especially when starting with units from our **Anime Story 2 beginner tier list**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What are the absolute best units for an *Anime Story 2 beginner tier list*?**
A: For beginners, Broly (Mythic) and Genos (Secret Evo) are highly recommended due to their strong early-game damage and accessibility. Ace (Legendary) is also a solid, underrated pick.

**Q: How do I get more gems and gold in *Anime Story 2*?**
A: Gems are primarily earned through achievements, daily challenges, and unit discovery. Gold is best farmed by repeatedly clearing the final boss stage of the highest world you can beat, which also drops valuable relic crafting materials.

**Q: Is it worth focusing on shiny units early on?**
A: Yes, shiny units deal more damage, making them a significant upgrade. If you acquire a shiny version of a good unit, prioritize investing in it. Raids are a good source for shiny variants of powerful units like Aizen.

**Q: What's the best way to improve my units' stats?**
A: Use Stat Chips to reroll stats. Focus on obtaining "Worthiness" for a higher chance of better stats. Additionally, ascending units increases their level cap, and evolving them (if applicable) provides substantial power boosts.
