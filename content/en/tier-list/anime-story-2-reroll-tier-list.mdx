## Why Trait Rerolls are Critical in Anime Story 2

In *Anime Story 2*, a unit's base stats are just the beginning. Traits act as powerful modifiers, significantly enhancing a unit's performance in combat. A perfectly rolled trait can transform a decent unit into an absolute powerhouse, dictating its viability across various game modes like raids, story missions, and infinite mode. Without optimized traits, even the strongest secret units might struggle against high-level content. This makes understanding and utilizing an effective **Anime Story 2 reroll tier list** not just beneficial, but essential for competitive play and efficient progression.

## Top-Tier Traits to Target in Anime Story 2

When it comes to an **Anime Story 2 reroll tier list**, certain traits consistently outperform others due to their universal utility or immense impact on specific unit types. While the "best" trait can sometimes be situational, a few stand out as must-haves for most high-tier units.

Here's a breakdown of the most sought-after traits:

*   **Berserk:** This trait is a game-changer for damage dealers, especially AoE units like Ace or Rimuru (Tempest). It significantly boosts damage output, allowing units to clear waves and bosses much faster. For early-game players, a Berserk Ace can be your ticket to AFK farming higher story stages.
*   **Monarch:** Crucial for units that rely on critical hits, Monarch ensures a guaranteed crit, which can be devastating when paired with high crit damage. This is particularly effective for units like Tengen when aiming for consistent burst damage.
*   **Shiny:** While not a trait in the traditional sense, having a shiny unit often comes with inherent stat bonuses and can unlock special abilities or enhanced versions of existing ones (e.g., White Lotus's maid outfit form). Farming for shiny units can be an indirect way to boost overall power.
*   **Crit Chance/Crit Damage Boosts:** For units without guaranteed crits, increasing their crit chance and crit damage through traits is vital. These traits can lead to massive damage spikes and are essential for maximizing DPS.

When considering your **Anime Story 2 reroll tier list**, always think about the unit's primary role. An AoE farmer benefits most from damage-boosting traits, while a single-target boss killer might prefer traits that enhance critical strikes or provide utility like enemy debuffs.

## Efficient Reroll Token Farming Strategies

Acquiring reroll tokens (also known as trait tokens) is the bottleneck for many players. Thankfully, *Anime Story 2* offers several consistent methods to stock up on these valuable items.

### Daily & Weekly Tasks

*   **Daily Quests:** Completing your daily quests is a simple and reliable way to earn a steady supply of reroll tokens. You typically get three quests per day, each contributing to your token count.
*   **Weekly Quests:** Don't forget the weekly quests! These often offer a larger payout of tokens, making them a crucial part of your farming routine.
*   **30-Minute Challenges:** These time-limited challenges often reward trait rerolls, so make sure to check them regularly and complete them whenever possible.

### Infinite Mode & Tower

*   **Infinite Mode:** This mode is an excellent source for farming units and, more importantly, reroll tokens. Players often find it to be one of the most optimal ways to get rerolls quickly. Focus on reaching stage 5, as this is where you gain a 2% chance for rerolls, which is a significant increase. Using strong AoE units like Tempest can help you progress further and faster.
*   **Tower:** The Tower is arguably one of the best sources of reroll tokens, especially when it resets. You earn five trait rerolls every five floors, essentially one reroll per floor cleared. Over time, this can accumulate into hundreds of tokens. Regularly clearing the Tower after a reset is highly recommended.

## Unit Synergy and Trait Selection

The best **Anime Story 2 reroll tier list** isn't just about individual traits; it's about how those traits synergize with your chosen units and overall team composition.

*   **Early Game Powerhouse (Ace):** For new players, Ace is a legendary unit that shines as an early-game AoE farmer. Equipping him with a Berserk trait and potentially crit gear (like the Baseball Bat or crit chance gear if you participated in certain events) allows him to solo nightmare story stages like Desert 2-3 or Beach Harbor, making him excellent for farming gems and gold.
*   **Endgame AoE Dominator (Rimuru/Tempest):** Once you reach the endgame, Rimuru (specifically his Tempest form) becomes an unparalleled AoE unit. Pairing him with a Berserk trait makes him the go-to unit for farming the most efficient stages for gems, coins, and XP, such as Heroic City Nightmare. His full AoE capabilities, combined with optimized stats, ensure quick and consistent clears.
*   **Support & Utility (Gojo, Destruction, Double Cyborg):**
    *   **Gojo:** A mythic support unit, Gojo is one of the best in the game due to his ability to double all damage-over-time effects. While not directly benefiting from damage traits, ensuring he has traits that boost his survivability or ability uptime can indirectly enhance your team's damage.

    *   **Double Cyborg:** Essential for farming slime tokens, Double Cyborg needs to be brought to secret rarity and a certain level to be effective. Traits that enhance his specific farming capabilities are key.

Remember, the goal is to build a balanced team where each unit's traits complement the others, covering various roles like AoE damage, single-target DPS, and support.
