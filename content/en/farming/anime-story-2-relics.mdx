<Section icon={Target} title="Crafting Your Relics: A Step-by-Step Guide">
Once you've gathered the required shards, the next step is to craft your desired relic at the **Upgrade Area**. This process is straightforward but requires careful attention to the materials and currency needed.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Access the Upgrade Area">
    Navigate to the **Upgrade Area** within the game. This is your central hub for all crafting and enhancement needs.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Select Relic Crafting">
    Within the Upgrade Area, locate and select the "Relic Crafting" option. You'll see a list of available relics and their crafting requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Gather Materials">
    Ensure you have the necessary **shards** and **Yen** (in-game currency) for the relic you wish to craft. For example, the Kana Relic requires 100 Orange Shards and ¥100,000.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Confirm Crafting">
    Once all requirements are met, confirm the crafting process. The relic will then be added to your inventory, ready to be equipped.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Equip to Unit">
    Go to your unit management screen and equip the newly crafted relic to the unit you want to boost. Remember, each unit can only hold one relic.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Crafting Checklist">
Before attempting to craft a relic, always double-check your inventory for the correct number of shards and sufficient Yen. This saves time and prevents wasted trips to the Upgrade Area.
</Callout>
