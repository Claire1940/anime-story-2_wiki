## Essential Daily & Weekly Reward Sources

Before diving into the high-yield strategies, it's vital to cover the consistent, lower-effort methods that provide a baseline of rewards. These daily and weekly activities are often overlooked but contribute significantly to your overall progress.

### Codes, Daily Rewards, and Quests

One of the easiest ways to kickstart your reward collection, especially for new players, is by redeeming codes. These often provide a substantial amount of trait rerolls and other resources. While not a recurring farming method, always check for new codes as they release.

Beyond codes, the game offers several consistent sources:

*   **Daily Rewards:** Simply logging in regularly grants rewards. Every six days, you typically receive 25 trait rerolls. While not a massive amount, it's a passive gain that adds up over time.
*   **Quests:** Both daily and weekly quests offer a small number of trait rerolls upon completion. These are usually straightforward tasks that you might complete naturally as you play.
*   **Level Rewards:** As you level up your account, *Anime Story 2* provides five trait rerolls every five levels. While the amount per level is modest, it's a nice bonus for continuous play.

These foundational methods ensure you're always accumulating some rewards, even if you're not actively engaged in intensive farming.

### Challenges: A Stepping Stone to Greater Rewards

Challenges in *Anime Story 2* offer a tiered approach to earning rewards. While some might seem underwhelming initially, they serve as a good stepping stone.

*   **Normal Challenges:** These are generally easy to complete, especially for mid-game players. However, they typically only yield one trait reroll, which many players find to be a very low return for the effort. Developers could certainly improve this payout.
*   **Daily Challenges:** A more lucrative option, daily challenges offer around 7-8 trait rerolls, along with gems and stars needed for unit evolution. For mid-level players, these are often manageable and provide a decent daily boost. New players might find them challenging initially, but they become a reliable source as your team strengthens.

While the individual rewards from normal and daily challenges might not seem like much, consistently tackling them contributes to your overall trait reroll count.

## The Battle Pass & Raids: Investing for Rewards

For those looking to accelerate their progress or who have built a strong roster, the Battle Pass and Raids offer more significant reward opportunities.

### Battle Pass Benefits

The Battle Pass in *Anime Story 2* provides a tiered reward system. While it costs 799 Robux, which can be a bit steep, it offers a considerable amount of trait rerolls over its duration. Players can expect to earn anywhere from 150 to 300 trait rerolls per Battle Pass cycle, alongside other valuable items. Even without purchasing the premium track, the free tiers still offer some rewards, making it a worthwhile system to engage with.

### Raids: High Risk, High Reward

Raids are an endgame activity designed for powerful teams. These battles are tough, often featuring bosses with hundreds of thousands of HP. Successfully completing raids can yield 25 trait rerolls per specific raid section in the shop. However, the drop chance for individual rerolls during the raid itself can be as low as 0.75%, making it less reliable for direct farming.

Raids require a strong team composition and strategic play. New players will likely struggle, but for those with powerful units like Broly or other OP characters, raids can be a good source of rewards. Remember, raids scale in difficulty, with later stages featuring bosses with 500k HP or more.

## Top-Tier Farming Methods: Where the Real Gains Are

Now, let's get to the strategies that truly stand out as the **anime story 2 best farming method** for trait rerolls. These methods require a capable team but offer the highest returns.

### The Slime Raid: A Consistent Contender

The Slime Raid is a highly recommended farming spot, especially for mid-to-late game players. While it only gives one trait reroll per run, its true value lies in the Slime Tokens it drops. These tokens can be used for summoning banners, where you can acquire stat chips, mythic orbs, and secret orbs. Accumulating 50 secret orbs can even grant you a boost and a secret unit.

The Slime Raid can be challenging, and even strong players might occasionally lose due to RNG. For beginners, it's advisable to check the *Anime Story 2* Discord server's tier list channel for recommended beginner teams. Units like Broly and Greed are often highlighted for their effectiveness. With a well-optimized team, you can efficiently clear the Slime Raid, often two-shotting the boss, and accumulate tokens and a steady stream of trait rerolls.

### The Tower Event: The Undisputed King of Reroll Farming

Based on community feedback and current game mechanics, the Tower Event is considered the absolute **anime story 2 best farming method** for trait rerolls. This event offers insane rewards for progressing through its difficult stages.

Here's why the Tower Event is so effective:

*   **Massive Bonus Rewards:** You receive an extra 100 trait rerolls every 250 floors you complete. The tower loops every 250 floors, meaning this bonus can be repeatedly earned.
*   **Consistent Floor Rewards:** Every fifth floor you clear grants you five trait rerolls. This provides a steady trickle of rewards as you climb.
*   **Limited Units & Shiny Chance:** Every 100 floors, you have a chance to obtain limited units and a five-shiny chance, adding more value to your progression.
*   **Scalable Difficulty:** While the tower becomes very difficult near the end of each loop, it's designed to be tackled by players of varying strengths. As you get stronger, you can push further, earning more rewards.

The Tower Event resets with major game updates (typically every 2.5 updates), allowing players to re-engage with the challenge and earn fresh rewards. This continuous cycle makes it an unparalleled source for trait rerolls. If you're serious about optimizing your trait reroll gains, focusing on the Tower Event should be your top priority.

## Store Bundles: A Premium Option

While not a farming method in the traditional sense, the in-game store offers bundles that can provide a significant number of trait rerolls for Robux. For players willing to spend, these bundles can offer a quick boost. For instance, Bundle Six often provides 250 trait rerolls and various boost items for 250 Robux, offering better value than the Battle Pass in terms of raw rerolls per Robux spent.

However, be cautious with bundles that offer unevolved units, such as the Shiny Greed. While the evolved form might be powerful, the unevolved version is often not worth the cost if you don't have the necessary evolution materials (like cups from World Seven or Eight). Always prioritize bundles that directly give you the rewards you need or units you can immediately utilize. Remember, you can absolutely progress in *Anime Story 2* without spending money on bundles.
