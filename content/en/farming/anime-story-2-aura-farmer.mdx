<Section icon={Shield} title="Passive Abilities and Advanced Tactics">
The Aura Farmer's true power emerges through its passive abilities, particularly those unlocked at higher upgrade levels. These abilities provide significant utility, damage amplification, and crowd control, making it a versatile unit for various combat scenarios.

**Key Passive Abilities:**

- **Psybeam Laser [UPG 5]:** Changes the attack type to **AoE (Line)**, drastically improving its ability to clear lanes of enemies. This is a crucial upgrade for wave management.
- **Unlocked Potential [UPG 9]:** At Upgrade 9, Aura Farmer unlocks its full potential, gaining powerful buffs.
- **Giant Pillar Smash [UPG 9]:** This ability is a game-changer.
  - **Execute** all non-Boss enemies, providing immediate clear.
  - Deals **(Combined Damage of units in Range x 150)** to all Boss-type enemies.
  - **Timestops** all enemies in range for **100s**.
  - Deals **(Damage x Aura)** every **10s** while Aura is consumed.
  - When Aura reaches **0** stacks, the unit resumes attacking.
  - Deals **10%** Damage in range every second.
  - Gains a flat Damage buff equal to **1%** of the damage dealt by this passive (Cap: **150B**).
  - Gains **5%** of Energy as a flat Damage buff and **7.5%** of Power as a flat Crit Damage buff.
  - Follows up any attack made by 'Unleashed Beast' three additional times.

<Callout type="info" title="Synergy with Other Units">
Units that can provide additional **Energy** or **Power** buffs will further enhance Aura Farmer's damage and crit damage, especially after unlocking its full potential.
</Callout>
