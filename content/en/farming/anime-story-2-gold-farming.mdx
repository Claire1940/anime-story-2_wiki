### Early Game Gold Farming: Ace Your Way to Riches

For new players in *Anime Story 2*, establishing a consistent gold and gem income is paramount. The legendary unit Ace stands out as an exceptional early-game farmer due to his full Area of Effect (AoE) attacks. Even at level 50, a well-equipped Ace can comfortably clear early story maps on Nightmare difficulty, providing a steady stream of gems and coins.

**Recommended Early-Game Strategy:**

*   **Unit of Choice:** Ace (especially with the Berserk trait).
*   **Farming Location:** Start with early story maps like "Desert 2-3" or "Beach Harbor." Experiment to find the highest Nightmare difficulty map Ace can consistently solo.
*   **Gear:** Equip Ace with a Baseball Bat and crit chance gear to maximize his damage output.
*   **Method:** Set Ace to AFK on the chosen Nightmare map. The boss of each stage typically drops more gold, making these runs highly rewarding for coins. Aim for maps where Ace can one-shot enemies on his first attack for maximum efficiency.

By focusing on Ace early on, you can accumulate enough resources to summon more powerful units and progress further into the game. This foundational **Anime Story 2 gold farming** method ensures a smooth start for any beginner.

### End Game Gold Farming: Tempest and Tengen Domination

Once you've advanced beyond the early stages, end-game **Anime Story 2 gold farming** shifts to maximizing returns from higher-level content. For experienced players, units like Rimuru (Tempest) and Tengen become indispensable for their superior AoE capabilities and efficiency in challenging stages.

**Optimal End-Game Farming Locations & Units:**

*   **Hero City (Nightmare):** This is widely considered the best stage for end-game farming. It offers a significant amount of gems (around 285-295), coins (over 465,000), and XP (7,500) per run on Nightmare difficulty.
*   **Recommended Units:**
    *   **Rimuru (Tempest):** Tempest is a full AoE unit that can solo Hero City Nightmare with ease. Unlike some other units, Tempest doesn't take damage, preventing ties and ensuring consistent clears. With SSS stats, Tempest can clear the stage quickly and efficiently.
    *   **Tengen:** Tengen is another viable option, especially when equipped with crit gear. While he might not one-shot every enemy like a perfectly statted Tempest, he can still solo Hero City Nightmare effectively, particularly with Monarch for guaranteed critical hits.
*   **VIP Bonus:** If you're serious about maximizing your gains, consider getting the VIP pass. It significantly boosts your coin income, with players reporting over 580,000 coins per run on Hero City Nightmare with VIP.

Consistently farming Hero City Nightmare with a powerful AoE unit like Tempest or Tengen is the most effective end-game strategy for **Anime Story 2 gold farming**. This method provides a steady stream of all three crucial resources: gems, coins, and XP.

### Maximizing Rerolls (Trait Tokens)

Rerolls are essential for optimizing your units' traits, allowing you to fine-tune their performance and create specialized builds. While farming gems and gold is important, don't overlook the various ways to acquire these valuable tokens.

**Key Sources for Rerolls:**

1.  **Infinite Mode:** Every stage in Infinite mode rewards 125 gems and provides opportunities for rerolls. It's an excellent way to farm units and, by extension, rerolls. For new players, reaching stage 5 and then letting your team die can be an optimal way to get a 2% chance for rerolls.
2.  **Daily Quests:** Completing your daily quests consistently is a simple and reliable source of rerolls.
3.  **30-Minute Challenges:** These timed challenges frequently offer trait rerolls as rewards.
4.  **Coin Shop:** The Coin Shop often has bundles or individual trait rerolls available for purchase.
5.  **Raids:** Participating in raids is one of the best daily methods for acquiring rerolls. Many raids offer multiple rewards of 25 trait rerolls, adding up quickly. Additionally, farming shiny units in raids can also drop rerolls with a 1% chance, sometimes even two per run.
6.  **Weekly Quests:** Don't forget your weekly quests, which can provide a substantial amount of rerolls (around 15).
7.  **Tower Mode:** This mode is a fantastic source of rerolls upon reset. You receive five rerolls for every five floors cleared, meaning essentially one reroll per floor. The Tower also offers significant gold and gem rewards, making it a highly efficient activity.
8.  **Battle Pass:** For both early and late-game players, the Battle Pass often provides a generous amount of gems and trait tokens, making it a worthwhile investment.
9.  **Live Milestones & Index:** Keep an eye on live milestones and unit index completion, as these can also reward rerolls.

By combining these methods, players can easily accumulate over 100 rerolls daily, ensuring you always have enough to perfect your unit builds. Effective **Anime Story 2 gold farming** extends beyond just coins and gems; a steady supply of rerolls is equally critical for achieving peak performance.

### Essential Tips for Efficient Farming

Beyond specific locations and units, several general tips can significantly boost your **Anime Story 2 gold farming** efficiency:

*   **Unit Stats:** Invest in optimizing your unit's stats, especially for your primary farmers like Ace or Tempest. SSS stats make a noticeable difference in clear speeds and consistency. Use stat chips acquired from events or banner summons to achieve this.
*   **Crit Chance Gear:** For units that benefit from critical hits (which is most damage dealers), prioritize crit chance gear. This can drastically increase your damage output and speed up farming runs.
*   **AFK Farming:** Leverage the game's AFK capabilities, especially overnight. Set up your best farming team on the most lucrative stage you can consistently clear on Nightmare difficulty.
*   **Community Engagement:** Join the *Anime Story 2* Discord server or other community groups. Players often share new farming strategies, optimal team compositions, and event information that can give you an edge.
*   **Events:** Always participate in in-game events. They frequently offer bonus gems, coins, stat chips, and rerolls, providing a temporary but significant boost to your resources.
