### Early Game Farming Strategies: Setting Up for Success

For new players starting their Anime Story 2 journey, focusing on reliable and accessible farming methods is key. Your initial goal should be to establish a stable income of gold and gems while slowly building up your unit roster.

*   **Story Mode Progression:** The primary way to earn initial gems and gold is by progressing through the story maps. Each successful completion, especially on higher difficulties like Nightmare, yields a good amount of rewards.
*   **Ace: The Early Game MVP:** According to player feedback, Ace is consistently highlighted as the best legendary unit for early-game farming due to his full Area of Effect (AoE) attacks. Even at level 50, a well-equipped Ace can easily AFK farm maps like Desert 2-3 on Nightmare difficulty, providing a steady stream of gems. If you can obtain the "Berserk" trait for Ace, his efficiency skyrockets.
*   **Identifying Your Optimal Story Map:** Experiment with different story maps to find the highest difficulty you can consistently clear with your current units, particularly with Ace. Farming this map overnight can significantly boost your early-game resources.
*   **Infinite Mode for Rerolls:** Infinite mode is an excellent source for unit farming and, more importantly, rerolls. While early players might struggle to clear many waves, aiming to reach at least stage 5 is beneficial. Some community strategies suggest finding a team that can clear the first five stages and then intentionally lose to quickly restart and capitalize on the 2% chance for rerolls.

### Maximizing Trait Rerolls and Other Daily Gains

Beyond specific farming locations, several daily and weekly activities contribute significantly to your resource pool, especially for trait rerolls.

*   **Daily Quests:** Always complete your daily quests. They consistently provide a steady stream of trait rerolls and other minor rewards.
*   **Weekly Quests:** Weekly quests offer a more substantial payout, typically around 15 rerolls, making them a crucial part of your weekly routine.
*   **30-Minute Challenges:** These time-gated challenges are another reliable source for trait rerolls.
*   **Coin Shop:** Don't forget to check the Coin Shop regularly. It often has trait rerolls available for purchase using your farmed gold.
*   **Tower Resets:** The Tower offers a unique opportunity for rerolls. Every five floors you clear grant five trait rerolls, effectively one reroll per floor. When the Tower resets, you can re-clear it for even more tokens, along with gold and gems. Players have reported accumulating hundreds of rerolls from Tower completions over time.
*   **Battle Pass:** The Battle Pass, especially for early-game players, is a treasure trove of gems and trait tokens. Actively progressing through it will yield consistent rewards.

Having the right units is only half the battle; optimizing them is the other.

For AoE units, increasing critical chance and critical damage can lead to one-shot clears, drastically improving efficiency.
