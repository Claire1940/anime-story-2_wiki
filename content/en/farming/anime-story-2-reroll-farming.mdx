## High-Efficiency Farming: The Tower Event Strategy

According to seasoned players, the Tower Event is currently the absolute best method for **anime story 2 reroll farming**. The Tower is designed as a gauntlet that tests your team's synergy and power, but the rewards are well worth the effort. The Tower operates on a loop system, typically consisting of 250 floors. As you climb, the difficulty scales significantly, but so do the payouts.

The reward structure for the Tower is highly lucrative. You receive five trait rerolls every five floors you successfully clear. However, the real prize comes at the end of the loop; completing all 250 floors grants a massive bonus of 100 trait rerolls. Because the Tower now loops every 250 floors, high-level players can repeatedly farm these stages to accumulate hundreds of rerolls in a single session. It is important to note that the Tower resets every few updates, so you must maximize your progress before the next reset occurs.

To succeed in the higher floors of the Tower, you need a team that can handle massive health pools. Many players in the community suggest using units like Broly or Greed, as their damage output scales well against the increasingly difficult mobs. If you are struggling, checking the official Discord for the latest tier lists is a highly recommended step before committing your resources to a specific team composition.

## Raids and Daily Challenges: Consistent Gains

While the Tower is the king of volume, Raids and Daily Challenges provide the essential daily "upkeep" for your **anime story 2 reroll farming** routine. The Daily Challenge is a mid-difficulty activity that grants eight trait rerolls upon completion, along with gems and stars. Stars are particularly important because they are required to evolve your units, and an evolved unit with a mediocre trait is often better than an unevolved unit with a great one.

Raids offer a different approach to farming. By participating in Raids, you earn Raid Tokens which can be spent in the Raid Shop. The shop typically stocks batches of 25 trait rerolls that can be purchased once you have cleared specific milestones. Furthermore, there is a small passive chance (approximately 0.75%) to receive a trait reroll as a direct drop from beating a Raid boss. While the drop rate is low, the combined value of the shop rewards and the potential drops makes Raids a staple of any efficient farming loop.

For players who prefer a more "AFK" or low-intensity experience, the Normal Challenge is available. It only rewards a single reroll per completion, which many players find underwhelming. However, if you are just short of a few rerolls and have a team strong enough to clear it in seconds, it can be a quick fix. Just be aware that it is significantly less time-efficient than focusing on the Tower or Daily Challenges.

## The Slime Raid and Specialized Rewards

The Slime Raid (or Slime Stage) represents the "second-best" active farming method currently available. This mode provides a unique currency known as Slime Tokens. While the direct reward for clearing the stage is often just one trait reroll, the real value lies in the Slime Banner. By spending your tokens on the banner, you have a chance to pull for stat chips, mythic orbs, and secret orbs.

Secret orbs are highly coveted by the community. If you manage to collect 50 of them, you can unlock powerful boosts and secret units that are otherwise nearly impossible to obtain. The Slime Boss itself has a massive health pool—often exceeding 200,000 HP in earlier stages and scaling much higher later on. To farm this effectively, your team must be optimized for burst damage.

Based on community feedback on Discord, the "Greed Fox" (the evolved version of Greed) is one of the most effective units for this specific raid. However, a common mistake players make is buying the Greed bundle without realizing that the unit must be evolved using a "Cup" found in World 7 or 8. Without the evolution, the unit's performance drops significantly. Always ensure you have the materials to evolve a unit before spending your hard-earned tokens or Robux on it.

## Premium vs. Free-to-Play: Navigating the Shop

Anime Story 2 is quite accessible for free-to-play players, but for those looking to speed up their **anime story 2 reroll farming**, the shop offers several options. The Battle Pass, priced at 799 Robux, is a common choice. It provides a mix of rerolls, gems, and exclusive units. Over the course of a full pass, you can expect to earn between 150 and 300 trait rerolls. While not the most efficient in terms of "Robux per reroll," the added value of the exclusive units makes it a solid investment for dedicated players.

If you are looking for pure volume, specific bundles are often a better deal. For example, Bundle 6 is frequently cited as a high-value option, offering 250 trait rerolls for 250 Robux. This 1:1 ratio is significantly better than the Battle Pass if your only goal is to fix a character's traits. However, as mentioned previously, be cautious with bundles that include unevolved units, as the time required to find the evolution materials might outweigh the immediate benefit of the rerolls.

Ultimately, the best way to progress is a hybrid approach. Use the free methods like the Tower and Daily Challenges for your primary income, and save your Robux for high-value bundles when you are chasing a specific "Secret" trait that requires hundreds of attempts to hit.
