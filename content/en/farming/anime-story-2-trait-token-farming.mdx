### Early Game Trait Token Farming: Setting Up for Success

For new players just starting their journey in *Anime Story 2*, establishing a consistent method for resource generation is key. While direct trait token farming might seem out of reach initially, focusing on units that can efficiently clear story maps for gems and gold will indirectly fuel your reroll attempts.

One of the best early-game units for this purpose is Ace. Known for his full AoE attacks, Ace can single-handedly clear many early story stages, even on Nightmare difficulty. For instance, Desert 2-3 is a fantastic spot for new players to AFK farm. With Ace, even if you only have a basic legendary trait like Berserk, you can consistently earn gems and gold. The more gems you accumulate, the more opportunities you'll have to summon for new units and potentially acquire trait tokens through events or banner pulls.

To optimize your early-game farming with Ace:
*   **Focus on AoE Damage:** Ace excels at clearing waves quickly, making him ideal for maps with many enemies.
*   **Prioritize Story Maps:** Identify the highest Nightmare difficulty story map Ace can reliably solo. This will yield the best gem and gold rewards.
*   **Overnight AFK:** Consider leaving your game running overnight on an easy-to-clear Nightmare stage to accumulate a significant amount of resources.

Remember, while this isn't direct *Anime Story 2 trait token farming*, it builds the foundation for future reroll acquisition by providing the currency needed for summons and other token-generating activities.

### Endgame Strategies for Maximizing Trait Rerolls

Once you've progressed into the endgame, your options for *Anime Story 2 trait token farming* expand significantly. Endgame players can leverage powerful units and tackle more challenging content to secure a higher volume of rerolls.

**1. Optimized Story Map Farming with Rimuru (Tempest)**

For endgame players, Rimuru (Tempest) stands out as an exceptional unit for farming gems, gold, and XP on Nightmare difficulty. Unlike some other units that might take damage and risk a loss, Tempest's full AoE capabilities ensure swift and safe clears.

A highly recommended stage for this is Hero City. On Nightmare, it offers impressive rewards:
*   **Gems:** Approximately 285-295 per run.
*   **Gold:** Around 465,000 per run.
*   **XP:** Roughly 7,500 per run.

Tempest's ability to clear this map quickly and efficiently makes it a prime candidate for continuous farming. Even with SSS stats, Tempest consistently solos this stage, providing a steady stream of resources that can be converted into trait tokens. If you have VIP, your gold earnings will be even higher, sometimes reaching over 580,000 per run.

**2. Infinite Mode for Consistent Rerolls**

Infinite Mode is a reliable source for trait tokens, offering 10 trait rerolls every five stages you clear. This mode also drops units, making it a dual-purpose farming location. The key is to find a team that can consistently clear at least five stages. For new players, reaching stage 5 and then letting your team die can be an optimal strategy, as it guarantees a 2% chance for a unit drop (which often comes with rerolls). Endgame players, especially with powerful AoE units like Tempest, can push much further, accumulating more rerolls per run.

**3. Daily Activities and Challenges**

Don't underestimate the power of consistency. Several daily and weekly activities provide a significant number of trait tokens:
*   **Daily Quests:** Completing your daily quests typically rewards you with 3 trait rerolls.
*   **Weekly Quests:** The weekly quests offer a larger bounty, usually around 15 trait rerolls.
*   **30-Minute Challenges:** These challenges often provide additional trait rerolls upon completion.
*   **Coin Shop:** Keep an eye on the Coin Shop, as it frequently offers bundles of 10 trait rerolls for purchase.

By diligently completing these tasks, you can easily net dozens of rerolls each day, contributing significantly to your *Anime Story 2 trait token farming* efforts.

### Raids: A Goldmine for Trait Tokens

Raids are arguably one of the most lucrative sources for trait tokens, especially for endgame players. They offer substantial rewards, and with optimized teams, you can clear them quickly and repeatedly.

*   **Daily Raid Rewards:** Participating in raids daily can yield a significant number of trait tokens. Many raids offer 25 trait rerolls as a reward, and with multiple raid types available, these can quickly add up.
*   **Shiny Unit Farming:** When farming for shiny units in raids, there's an additional bonus: a 1% chance to drop 2 trait rerolls per run. Some raids can be completed in as little as 3 seconds, making this an incredibly efficient method for passive reroll generation while pursuing rare unit variants.

To maximize raid efficiency for *Anime Story 2 trait token farming*:
*   **Assemble a Strong Raid Team:** Focus on units with high damage and AoE capabilities to clear raids quickly.
*   **Prioritize Quick Clears:** The faster you complete raids, the more runs you can squeeze into a farming session, increasing your chances of token drops.
*   **Monitor Raid Schedules:** Stay informed about which raids are active and what rewards they offer.

### Tower Resets: A Hidden Gem for Rerolls

The Tower mode offers a unique opportunity for *Anime Story 2 trait token farming* every time it resets. For every five floors you clear in the Tower, you receive five trait rerolls. This essentially translates to one trait reroll per floor. Players who consistently climb the Tower can accumulate hundreds of rerolls during a single reset cycle.

Beyond trait tokens, the Tower also provides a substantial amount of gold and gems, making it a highly rewarding activity. Make sure to prioritize clearing the Tower whenever it resets to capitalize on these valuable rewards.

### Battle Pass and Milestones

Don't overlook the Battle Pass as a consistent source of trait tokens, especially for early-game players. Both the free and premium tracks often include trait tokens as rewards for progressing through tiers. Similarly, checking live milestones and your in-game index can sometimes reveal additional, unexpected sources of rerolls or related resources.

### FAQ

**Q: What is the fastest way to get trait tokens in *Anime Story 2*?**
A: The fastest way for endgame players is often through raids, especially those that can be cleared in seconds, offering a chance for 2 trait rerolls per run, combined with daily raid rewards and consistent Tower resets. For new players, Infinite Mode (clearing to stage 5) and daily quests are efficient.

**Q: Which units are best for *Anime Story 2 trait token farming*?**
A: For early game, Ace is highly recommended due to his full AoE. For endgame players, Rimuru (Tempest) is a top choice for efficient farming of gems and gold, which indirectly leads to more trait tokens, and other powerful AoE units capable of quick raid clears are also excellent.

**Q: Can I AFK farm trait tokens in *Anime Story 2*?**
A: While you can AFK farm gems and gold on story maps with units like Ace or Rimuru, which indirectly helps with trait token acquisition, direct AFK farming of trait tokens is less common. However, leaving Infinite Mode running to clear stages in the background can passively earn some.

**Q: How many trait rerolls can I get daily in *Anime Story 2*?**
A: By combining daily quests, 30-minute challenges, Coin Shop purchases, raid rewards, and consistent Infinite Mode runs, players can realistically obtain around 100 trait rerolls or more per day.
