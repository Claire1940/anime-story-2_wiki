### Story Mode: Your Foundation for Early Farming

Story mode in *Anime Story 2* isn't just for narrative progression; it's a vital early-game resource farm. Focusing on units with Area of Effect (AoE) abilities can significantly speed up your runs and maximize your gains.

A standout unit for early story progression is **Ace**. With full AoE capabilities, Ace can easily be triple-S ranked in stats, particularly attack. Aim for around 6,000 to 6,500 takedowns to guarantee triple-S on all stats. A well-built Ace can carry you surprisingly far, potentially even up to the Soul Haven map.

For those lucky enough to pull **Beiru** from a secret orb, or even **Ten**, your farming potential expands greatly. Beiru can push your story progression even further, while Ten or Remu are ideal for farming the last map. This final stage offers the best all-in-one return for XP, gold, and gems, along with valuable shards. Tempest is another strong contender for efficient farming on Nightmare difficulty.

For players prioritizing gems, a "bug" (as of the current version) exists in Act 3, Nightmare difficulty, specifically with a double Genos setup. While it sacrifices some gold and XP, it offers the fastest gem acquisition, completing stages in a rapid 2.3 to 2.5 seconds. For overall resource gathering, however, the last story map remains the top choice.

### Raids: Targeting High-Value Units and Resources

Raids in *Anime Story 2* are your go-to for specific unit shards and high-tier rewards. The key here is single-target DPS and strategic unit selection.

**Broly Rookie** is an excellent choice for raids due to his immense damage output, even without a full advantage. If you're looking to build a top-tier Slime team, acquiring a **Monarch** is highly recommended as it synergizes well with Broly.

However, the undisputed king of raid farming is **Eizen**. His ability to stun enemies at the start of a round, combined with high damage, makes him incredibly effective, often allowing for one-shot kills on raid bosses. Pairing Eizen with **Beiru** can make runs even smoother, as Beiru doesn't require Monarch support to be effective. Another potent duo for raids involves **Beiru** and **Gojo**, with Gojo's damage-over-time amplification leading to quick kills, typically within 6-8 seconds once poison is applied.

For optimal raid farming, focus on your best single-target DPS units. Equipping units like Eizen with a baseball bat relic can further boost their damage, leading to more consistent one-shots. If a boss doesn't go down in one hit, a secondary DPS unit can quickly finish the job. Remember to constantly check and optimize your unit stats to ensure maximum efficiency.

### Slime Apocalypse: The Consistent Resource Goldmine

The Slime Apocalypse event is a fantastic source of consistent resources, and a specific team composition can make farming it incredibly efficient. Shoutout to Acid from the community Discord for developing this powerful setup.

The core of this team revolves around:
*   **Shiny Ultimate Warrior:** A must-have, dealing 2x damage against elemental types (Slimes are water).
*   **Bond:** One-shots enemies under 25% HP, crucial for quick clears.
*   **Cyborg (x2):** This unit is unique. At level 193, Cyborg has just enough health to die quickly and detonate, dealing massive AoE damage to clear the slimes. Leveling him too high will prevent him from dying and ruin the run.
*   **Tatsumiki:** Provides a significant 30% damage boost to the team.
*   **Rookie:** Acts as a tank, absorbing attacks.

This team composition typically clears the Slime Apocalypse in 9-12 seconds, making it one of the most consistent and fastest farming methods in *Anime Story 2*. Broly often one-shots the initial waves, with Bond stepping in if necessary, leading to rapid completion and substantial rewards.

### Tower Mode: Navigating the RNG and Preparing for Updates

The Tower in *Anime Story 2* is a true test of your unit roster and a significant source of powerful rewards, including guaranteed Sid every 100 floors and a 5% chance for a shiny unit. However, as many top players will attest, the current Tower meta heavily relies on RNG, especially with impending updates that can drastically shift unit viability.

**Essential Units for Tower Progression:**

*   **Eizen:** An absolute must-have. His ability to stun three enemies at the start of a round for one turn is game-changing. Combined with his 200% damage on the first attack, he can often one-shot critical threats.
*   **Itachi:** Another crucial unit. His 25% chance to avoid damage and counterattack, along with a guaranteed miss on the next attack after being hit, introduces a vital layer of RNG protection.
*   **Cyborg:** While controversial, Cyborg can be effective. If enemies have AoE units that don't instantly wipe your team, Cyborg's death explosion can clear the field, allowing your remaining units to finish. However, if he dies without clearing enough units, it can lead to a loss.
*   **Sound Breather:** A high-risk, high-reward unit. If his combo lands, he can decimate enemy teams, but his inconsistency makes him a gamble.
*   **Slime:** Provides a flat 10% damage buff and makes enemies take 10% more damage, making him a valuable support.
*   **Beiru:** A strong all-rounder that can be slotted into many teams.

**Team Composition and Strategy:**

The Tower meta is fluid, and your team composition often needs to be adjusted based on the enemy lineup. For instance, facing an enemy Itachi can be problematic due to his passive, making units like Rumu vulnerable. Similarly, an enemy Eizen can stun your key damage dealers, disrupting your strategy.

The key to success in the Tower is often finding the right balance of stuns, damage mitigation, and burst damage. Units like **Ten** and **Tempest** can provide significant DPS. While having Monarch units certainly helps, even without them, strategic unit placement and a bit of luck with RNG can lead to victories.

It's important to remember that the Tower is an RNG fest. Sometimes, despite the best units and strategy, the dice just don't roll your way. However, understanding the strengths of these core units and how their abilities interact with enemy compositions will significantly improve your chances.

### Optimizing Your Farming Strategy

To truly master the *Anime Story 2* farming and Tower progression, consider these overarching tips:

1.  **Prioritize Unit Stats:** Always aim for triple-S stats on your main farming and Tower units. Attack, Crit Range, and Crit Damage are usually paramount.
2.  **Relic Management:** Equip appropriate relics. The baseball bat, for example, is excellent for boosting single-target damage on units like Eizen in raids.
3.  **Stay Updated:** The game's meta changes frequently with updates. Keep an eye on community discussions (like the official *Anime Story 2* Discord) and creator content for the latest strategies and unit tier lists.
4.  **Resource Allocation:** Decide whether you need gems, gold, or XP most, and tailor your farming activity accordingly. The last story map is great for all-in-one, while the Act 3 Nightmare "bug" is gem-focused.
5.  **Experiment:** Don't be afraid to try different unit combinations for the Tower. What works for one floor might not work for another. Sometimes a slight adjustment can make all the difference.
