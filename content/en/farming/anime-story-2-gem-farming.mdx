## Why Gems Are Essential in Anime Story 2

Gems in Anime Story 2 are more than just a currency; they are your gateway to unlocking the game's full potential. With a stacked banner often featuring meta-defining units like Secret rare characters (e.g., Destruction for support, Dark Assassin for early game, or the indispensable Broly for slime token farming), collecting gems is paramount. Mythic supports like Gojo, who doubles all damage-over-time effects, and powerful AoE units like Ace or Rimuru, are all within reach with sufficient gem reserves. Beyond summoning, gems can also facilitate faster progression through other in-game systems, making them a top priority for any serious player.

## Early Game Anime Story 2 Gem Farming Strategies

For new players, establishing a consistent gem income is vital. The key here is to leverage early-game units and accessible story stages.

### Ace: Your Early-Game Gem Farming Champion

One of the most recommended units for new players to focus on is Ace. As a full AoE (Area of Effect) legendary unit, Ace can solo many early story stages, even on Nightmare difficulty, allowing you to AFK farm for gems.

*   **Recommended Stage:** Desert (specifically 2-3) is a great starting point for new players. Ace, even at level 50 with a basic Berserk trait, can often clear this reliably.
*   **Progression:** As your Ace improves, test his limits on other early stages like Beach Harbor. The goal is to find the highest Nightmare difficulty story map he can consistently clear on his own. For instance, Ace can solo Nightmare on Rome 7, netting around 270 gems per run.
*   **AFK Farming:** Once you identify a suitable stage, you can leave the game running overnight to accumulate a significant amount of gems.

### Leveraging Daily and Weekly Quests

Don't underestimate the power of routine. Daily and weekly quests are a consistent source of gems, gold, and even reroll tokens. Make sure to complete these regularly as they add up over time.

### Infinite Mode for Consistent Drops

Infinite mode, while primarily known for farming units and rerolls, also provides a decent amount of gems. Each stage cleared can reward you with around 125 gems. While not the primary source, it's a good bonus while you're working on other objectives.

## Mid to End Game Anime Story 2 Gem Farming Optimization

As you progress and acquire more powerful units, your gem farming strategies can become significantly more efficient.

### Rimuru (Tempest): The Endgame Powerhouse

For endgame players, Rimuru (Tempest form) is widely considered the best unit for gem, coin, and XP farming. Unlike other AoE options like Sound Breather, Rimuru doesn't take damage, preventing tie situations and ensuring consistent clears.

*   **Optimal Stage:** The Hero City stage (Nightmare difficulty) is a prime location. A fully optimized Rimuru can clear this stage extremely quickly, yielding approximately 285 gems, 465,000 coins, and 7,500 XP per run.
*   **Setup:** Ensure your Rimuru has strong stats. Players often aim for SSS stats, focusing on critical chance and damage.
*   **VIP Benefits:** If you have VIP, you'll see even higher returns, with some players reporting over 580,000 coins per run on Hero City.

### Tower Resets: A Hidden Gem Mine

The Tower mode is an often-overlooked source of significant rewards. When the Tower resets, you receive a substantial amount of gems, gold, and reroll tokens. Each five floors completed typically grants five rerolls, effectively one reroll per floor cleared. This consistent influx of resources makes pushing the Tower a highly valuable activity.

### Battle Pass: Long-Term Investment

The Battle Pass, particularly for early-game players, offers a wealth of gems and trade tokens as you progress through its tiers. Consider it a long-term investment that provides steady rewards for your playtime.

## Maximizing Reroll Token Farming

While the focus is on **Anime Story 2 gem farming**, reroll tokens are equally important for optimizing your units.

*   **Infinite Mode (Stage 5):** This is one of the most optimal ways for new players to farm rerolls. Clear up to stage 5, then find a team that can consistently die after the fifth stage. This gives you a 2% chance for rerolls, a significant improvement from previous rates.
*   **Daily Challenges and Coin Shop:** Don't forget your daily challenges and the Coin Shop, which offer 10 reroll tokens each.
*   **Raids:** Raids are an excellent source, providing 25 rerolls per run in certain instances. Additionally, farming shiny units in raids can drop rerolls with a 1% chance, sometimes even two per run.
*   **Tower:** As mentioned, Tower resets are a goldmine for rerolls, often yielding hundreds of tokens.
*   **Quests:** Daily quests provide three rerolls, and weekly quests offer around 15. Consistently completing these can net you over 100 rerolls daily.

## General Tips for Efficient Farming

*   **Unit Traits:** Pay attention to unit traits. Legendary traits like Berserk can significantly boost your farming efficiency.
*   **Gear Optimization:** Equip your farming units with appropriate gear. Crit chance gear, for example, can ensure faster clears.
*   **VIP Pass:** If you're serious about the game, the VIP pass offers substantial bonuses to gold and potentially other resources, greatly accelerating your farming.
*   **Events:** Always keep an eye on in-game events. Many events offer stat chips, gems, and other valuable items as rewards, especially for summoning on banners.
*   **Live Milestones & Index:** Don't forget to claim rewards from live milestones and completing your unit index. These often provide a one-time burst of gems and other goodies.

By consistently applying these strategies, you'll find your gem, gold, and reroll token reserves growing steadily. ## Frequently Asked Questions about Anime Story 2 Gem Farming

**Q: What is the fastest way to get gems in Anime Story 2 for new players?**
A: For new players, the fastest way to get gems is to consistently farm early story stages on Nightmare difficulty using an AoE unit like Ace. Find the highest stage Ace can solo and AFK farm it. Completing daily and weekly quests also provides a steady income.

**Q: How do endgame players maximize their Anime Story 2 gem farming?**
A: Endgame players should focus on using powerful AoE units like Rimuru (Tempest) to farm high-difficulty stages such as Hero City (Nightmare). This stage offers the best returns for gems, gold, and XP. Leveraging Tower resets and Battle Pass rewards is also crucial.

**Q: Are there any specific units recommended for efficient gem farming?**
A: Yes, for early game, Ace (legendary, full AoE) is highly recommended. For endgame, Rimuru (Tempest form, full AoE) is considered the top choice due to his damage output and survivability.

**Q: Besides gems, what other resources can I farm efficiently?**
A: Many of the same methods that yield gems also provide gold and reroll tokens. Infinite mode is excellent for rerolls, while high-difficulty story stages (like Hero City with Rimuru) provide massive amounts of gold and XP. Daily/weekly quests, raids, and tower resets are also great for all three resources.
