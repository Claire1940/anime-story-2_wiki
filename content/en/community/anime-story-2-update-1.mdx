### Mastering Story Mode Progression and Farming

Story mode is the foundation of your *Anime Story 2* journey, offering valuable XP, gold, and gems. For beginners, a strong AoE unit is crucial. Ace, with his full AoE capabilities, is an excellent choice and relatively easy to build for triple S stats in attack, crit range, and crit damage. With around 6,500 takedowns, Ace can guarantee triple S stats, allowing him to carry you through many early stages, potentially up to Soul Haven.

If you're lucky enough to acquire Beiru from a secret orb and utilize all available codes, your progression can accelerate significantly. Beiru, combined with Ace, can clear stages like the one that yields 277 gems per run, making gem farming much more efficient. For the absolute last map, which is ideal for all-in-one XP, gold, gems, and shard farming, units like Ten or Remu are highly recommended. Tempest also proves to be a strong contender for these later stages.

For those looking for a quick gem boost, a community-reported "bug" in Act 3 Nightmare, involving double Genos, can net you gems rapidly in 2.3-2.5 seconds per clear. While this method is faster for gems, it sacrifices some gold and XP, which are better obtained from the final story stages. Prioritizing full AoE units or focusing on specific units like Sid for stage 93 will ensure smooth story progression in *Anime Story 2*.

### Conquering Raids for Essential Resources

Raids in *Anime Story 2* are your go-to for specific unit drops and materials. While they can be tricky initially, certain units excel in these encounters. Broly Rookie is a solid choice due to his high damage output, even without full elemental advantage. If you're also building a team for the Slime Apocalypse, acquiring Monarch is highly beneficial, as Monarch also performs well in raids.

However, the undisputed king of raid farming is Eizen. His ability to stun enemies for one turn at the start of a round, combined with a 200% damage boost on his first attack, makes him invaluable. This often allows him to one-shot raid bosses. Pairing Eizen with Beiru can make runs even smoother, ensuring consistent kills. For subsequent raids, Eizen continues to be a powerhouse, often soloing bosses or requiring minimal support. It's crucial to ensure Eizen has a baseball bat equipped for maximum damage, especially for critical hits. Regularly checking your unit stats and optimizing them for damage rather than crit chance can significantly improve raid clear times. Itachi is another essential unit to acquire for raids, as his passive (25% chance to avoid damage and counterattack) becomes critical for later-game content like the Tower.

### Dominating the Slime Apocalypse Event

The Slime Apocalypse event in *Anime Story 2* offers fantastic rewards, and a specialized team composition can make all the difference. A highly effective strategy, credited to Discord user "Acid," focuses on specific unit synergies for rapid clears.

**Recommended Slime Apocalypse Team:**

*   **Shiny Ultimate Warrior:** A must-have. This unit deals 2x more damage against elemental units, and the slimes in this event are water-elemental.
*   **Bond:** Crucial for executing enemies under 25% HP, ensuring quick finishes.
*   **Cyborg (x2):** This unit is unique. At level 193, Cyborg has just enough health and damage to die quickly and detonate, dealing massive AoE damage to all slimes. Leveling Cyborg too high will prevent him from dying and ruin the strategy.
*   **Tatsumaki:** Provides a significant 30% damage boost to the team.
*   **Rookie:** Acts as a tank, absorbing initial attacks and stuns.

This team composition allows for incredibly fast clears, often completing the event in 9-12 seconds. Broly is also an excellent choice for one-shotting waves, with Bond serving as a reliable backup. This strategy has proven to be highly consistent for farming large amounts of currency, as evidenced by players accumulating tens of thousands of gems.

### Ascending the Tower of Trials

The Tower in *Anime Story 2* is a test of strategy and, frankly, a good deal of RNG. While the meta is expected to shift with *Anime Story 2 Update 1* and new units, certain core units remain essential.

**Key Tower Units:**

*   **Eizen:** An absolute must-have. His ability to stun three enemies at the start of a round for one turn is game-changing. Coupled with his 200% damage on the first attack, he can often one-shot critical threats.
*   **Itachi:** Another crucial unit. His passive provides a 25% chance to avoid damage and counterattack, making him incredibly resilient. After being hit, the next attack against Itachi misses, adding another layer of defense.
*   **Cyborg:** Controversial but potentially powerful. If the enemy team has AoE units that don't instantly wipe your team, Cyborg can die and detonate, clearing out enemy units. However, if your entire team dies, it's still a loss.
*   **Sound Breather:** A high-risk, high-reward unit. If he lands his combo, he can decimate enemies, but his effectiveness relies heavily on RNG.
*   **Slime:** Provides a flat 10% damage buff to your team and makes enemies take 10% more damage with his black flames.
*   **Beiru:** A strong support unit, often equipped with a baseball bat for increased damage.

The Tower meta is heavily RNG-dependent. Your team composition often needs to be adjusted based on the enemy lineup. For instance, facing enemy Itachis means your units will take more damage due to their passive. Similarly, enemy Eizens can stun your key units, disrupting your strategy. Units like Igoras can be devastating once they start rolling, making early elimination a priority. While a full Monarch team can certainly help, even without it, strategic unit placement and a bit of luck can lead to victory. Units like Tempest are also strong DPS options for the Tower. The goal is to reach floor 100, which guarantees a Sid unit and offers a 5% chance for a shiny.
