## Story Mode: The Foundation of Your Progress

Story mode is where every player begins their journey. It is the primary source of XP, Gold, and Gems. To progress efficiently through the early stages, you need a reliable "carry" unit.

### The Power of Ace
According to experienced players, **Ace** is one of the best starter units for Story mode. He is relatively easy to build and features full AoE (Area of Effect) attacks. To maximize his potential, players recommend equipping him with the **Baseball Bat** relic. 

Achieving "Triple S" stats on Ace—specifically in Attack and Crit Damage—is highly recommended. Community members suggest that reaching around 6,000 to 6,500 takedowns on a unit can guarantee a Triple S rating on stats, making Ace a powerhouse that can carry you all the way to the Soul Haven map.

### Efficient Gem Farming
If you are looking for the fastest way to accumulate gems after the **anime story 2 release**, there is a specific strategy involving Act 3 on Nightmare difficulty. By using double Genos units, players have discovered a "cheeky" method to clear the stage in under 2.5 seconds. While this method sacrifices some Gold and XP, it is widely considered the fastest way to farm the Gems needed for your next big summon.

For those who want a more balanced farm, the last map in the current rotation is the gold standard. Using units like **Tengen** or **Rimuru** (specifically for the final map) allows you to gain Shards, XP, Gold, and Gems simultaneously.

## Raid Strategies: Boss Slaying for Elite Rewards

Raids in Anime Story 2 are significantly more challenging than standard Story stages. They require high single-target DPS (Damage Per Second) and strategic stun mechanics.

### The Reign of Eisen
If there is one unit that has defined the **anime story 2 release** meta for raids, it is **Eisen**. Eisen is prized for his ability to stun bosses, effectively neutralizing their most dangerous attacks. When paired with a high-damage unit like **Rookie** or **Broly**, Eisen can facilitate "one-shot" builds that make raid farming trivial.

### DoT and Support Synergy
For players who haven't pulled Eisen yet, a combination of **Beiru** and **Gojo** is a popular alternative. This duo relies on Damage over Time (DoT) and poison mechanics. Once the poison stacks are applied, Gojo’s ability to double DoT damage can melt a boss’s health bar in 6 to 8 seconds. 

It is important to remember that for these raid builds, the **Monarch** trait is helpful but not always strictly necessary, especially if your units are high level and equipped with the right relics.

## Climbing the Tower: The Ultimate RNG Challenge

The Tower is the true endgame of Anime Story 2. Currently, the top players on the leaderboard admit that the Tower is heavily influenced by RNG (Random Number Generation). Because enemy passives apply just like yours do, a single unlucky stun or missed attack can end a high-level run.

### Essential Tower Units
To minimize the impact of RNG, you need units with powerful passives. **Eisen** remains a staple here because he stuns three enemies at the start of a round for one turn. This is arguably the most "broken" mechanic in the game currently.

Another mandatory unit is **Itachi**. His passive gives him a 25% chance to avoid damage and counterattack for 50% damage. More importantly, after being hit, the next attack against Itachi is guaranteed to miss. This creates a defensive loop that can save your team from powerful AoE bosses.

### The Role of Sound Breather and Slime
While controversial, **Sound Breather** is often used in high-level Tower climbs. He is a high-risk, high-reward unit who can decimate an entire enemy team if his combo lands. Additionally, the **Slime** unit provides a 10% damage buff and applies "Black Flames," which makes enemies take 10% more damage.

When facing difficult floors, especially those featuring double Eisen or Igoras, players often swap their relics to ensure their most durable units (like Itachi) have the highest HP to soak up hits, while their DPS units carry the Baseball Bat for maximum damage output.
