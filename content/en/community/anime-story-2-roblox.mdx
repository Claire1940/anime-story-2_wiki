### Unit Ascension, Traits, and Shiny Transfers

Ascending units is crucial for maximizing their potential. For legendary units like Ace, you'll need multiple copies to ascend them, significantly boosting their power. While this requires a substantial investment of gems, the increased damage output, especially for an AoE unit like Ace, makes it worthwhile for clearing content faster.

Traits are another layer of customization that can greatly enhance your units. Damage traits like Berserk are excellent for early-game units. For more advanced units, aiming for rainbow traits offers significant buffs. While some traits, like "Dead Eye," might not be ideal without specific critical gear, others can dramatically improve a unit's performance. The process of rerolling traits can be resource-intensive, but a well-rolled trait can make a huge difference.

Shiny units, identifiable by their visual flair, offer a 25% stat buff, making them highly desirable. If you acquire a shiny version of a unit you've already invested in, you can perform a "shiny transfer" for 50 million gold. While this might seem like a hefty sum, gold becomes abundant with efficient farming, making it a worthwhile investment to upgrade your core team. For example, getting a shiny Ace and transferring its stats can result in a triple S-tier unit.
