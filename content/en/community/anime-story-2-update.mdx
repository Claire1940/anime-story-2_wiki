### Mastering Early Game Progression and Essential Units

Starting strong in *Anime Story 2* can make all the difference. The initial hours are crucial for setting up a solid foundation for your account. One of the best ways to kickstart your progress is by utilizing any available codes to claim free gems, gold, and valuable items like slime tokens, ascend tickets, and rerolls. These resources are invaluable for acquiring your first strong units.

When it comes to early game unit acquisition, focus on powerful AoE (Area of Effect) units that can clear waves quickly. Based on player experience, legendary units like Ace are highly recommended due to their full AoE capabilities. For mythic units, Broly, Gojo, and Devil Cyborg are considered top-tier picks. While Beiru offers mark and poison, making him useful in the long run, Ten is often preferred for early game farming due to his efficiency.

Purchasing game passes like VIP, 3x (for faster grinding), and Shiny Hunter can significantly accelerate your progress. VIP offers a 25% coin and XP boost, while the 3x pass drastically speeds up grinding. Shiny Hunter is crucial because shiny units provide a 25% buff, making them incredibly desirable for optimizing your team as early as possible.

### Efficient Farming Strategies for Gems and Gold

Gold and gems are the lifeblood of progression in *Anime Story 2*. Without a steady supply, upgrading units, rolling for new ones, and advancing through the story become incredibly difficult. One highly effective early-game farming method involves AFK (away from keyboard) farming the desert map. This map is known to provide a substantial amount of gems, allowing you to quickly accumulate resources needed for summons and upgrades.

As you progress, completing story stages on Nightmare difficulty, especially Act 1, can yield significant gold and gems. Units like Whis, with their three AoE attacks, can be excellent for clearing these stages efficiently. The goal is to reach a point where you can consistently farm high-difficulty stages for maximum rewards.

Once you acquire a unit like Sid, a game-changing farming strategy becomes available. The "Sid technique" on the Hero City map, specifically against the double Devil Cyborgs, is a community-reported bug/feature that allows for immense overnight farming. Sid's attacks, for some reason, cause both cyborgs to explode, granting massive amounts of gems (around 285 per run) and gold (500k per run) with minimal effort. This method can generate millions of gems and billions of gold, enough for multiple pity summons and extensive unit upgrades.

### Optimizing Your Units: Traits, Ascensions, and Shinies

Beyond acquiring units, optimizing them is paramount. Traits play a significant role in enhancing a unit's performance. For damage dealers like Ace, aim for traits like Berserk or other damage-boosting rainbow traits. While rolling for specific rainbow traits can be challenging (a 2% chance, meaning roughly 50 rolls per desired trait), the investment is worthwhile for late-game content.

Ascending legendary units, while requiring multiple copies, significantly boosts their stats. Farming dupes of key units like Ace is a common strategy. Shiny units, as mentioned, provide a 25% buff, making them superior versions of their regular counterparts. The cost to shiny transfer is 50 million gold, which, while substantial early on, becomes manageable with efficient farming.

For mythic units, consider their specific roles. For example, Beiru's mark and poison can be very effective, especially when paired with units that benefit from debuffs. Ten, on the other hand, excels in farming due to his AoE capabilities. For events like the slime event, a shiny Broly can be incredibly useful, and pursuing a Monarch trait on him could further enhance his utility for faster runs. Remaru is also considered a universally strong unit for a Monarch trait, offering consistent value throughout the game.
