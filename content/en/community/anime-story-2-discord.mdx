### Conquering the Tower: RNG, Units, and Meta Shifts

The Tower in *Anime Story 2* is a true test of strategy and, as many players will attest, a significant amount of RNG. The **Anime Story 2 Discord** is where top players share their evolving meta strategies, especially with upcoming updates that frequently shift the landscape. While the meta is subject to change with new units, certain units are consistently discussed as essential for Tower progression.

*   **Eizen:** Considered a must-have for his ability to stun three enemies at the start of a round and deal 200% damage on his first attack. This can often lead to one-shotting weaker enemies or significantly crippling stronger ones.
*   **Itachi:** Another crucial unit due to his 25% chance to avoid damage and counterattack, along with a guaranteed miss on the next attack after being hit. His passive introduces a layer of RNG that can turn the tide of a battle.
*   **Cyborg:** A controversial but potentially game-changing unit. If timed correctly, his death explosion can wipe out enemy teams, especially if they lack the burst damage to eliminate your entire squad. However, as player feedback suggests, if he dies too early without taking out enough enemies, it can lead to a loss.
*   **Sound Breather:** While considered "suicidal" by some, his combo can decimate enemy teams if it lands, adding another high-risk, high-reward element to Tower runs.
*   **Slime:** Provides a crucial 10% damage buff and black flames that deal 66% damage, making enemies take 10% more damage. This support unit is often undervalued but can be vital for pushing higher floors.

The community emphasizes that Tower runs are often an "RNG fest," and team compositions need to be flexible based on the enemy lineup. Discussions on the **Anime Story 2 Discord** frequently revolve around adapting teams to counter specific enemy units like Itachi, Eizen, or Igoras, who can pose significant threats. The advice often boils down to praying for favorable RNG and switching units as needed. Remember, every 100 floors in the Tower offers a guaranteed Sid and a 5% chance for a shiny unit, providing strong incentives for continued progression.
