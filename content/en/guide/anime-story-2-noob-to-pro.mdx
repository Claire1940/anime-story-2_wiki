### Laying the Foundation: Early Game Essentials

Starting strong in *Anime Story 2* is paramount. Your initial decisions regarding unit summons and resource allocation will set the stage for your entire progression. The goal is to establish a solid foundation that allows for efficient grinding and story progression.

Upon creating a new account, leveraging available codes is your first priority. These codes often grant a significant boost in gems, gold, and even powerful units or reroll tokens. For example, a fresh account can start with a massive stash of gems, gold, slime tokens, ascend tickets, and rerolls, potentially guaranteeing a Monarch unit and a Secret unit right from the get-go. This initial influx of resources is critical for a swift *Anime Story 2 noob to pro* ascent.

When it comes to initial summons, prioritize units that offer strong Area of Effect (AoE) damage. Legendary units like Ace are highly recommended due to their full AoE capabilities, which are invaluable for clearing early story stages quickly. For Mythic units, focus on Broly, Gojo, and Devil Cyborg. These units provide excellent damage and utility that will carry you through tougher challenges. While some units like Beiru offer long-term benefits with debuffs like mark and poison, early-game farming often benefits more from raw damage output.

Game passes are another essential investment for any serious player. VIP status offers a 25% boost to coin and XP gain, while the 3x game pass triples your grinding speed. The Shiny Hunter pass is also crucial, as shiny units provide a 25% stat buff, making them highly desirable for optimizing your team. These passes aren't strictly necessary for free-to-play, but they significantly accelerate your progress towards becoming an *Anime Story 2 noob to pro*.

### Efficient Farming Strategies: Fueling Your Progression

To truly become an *Anime Story 2 noob to pro*, efficient farming is non-negotiable. You'll need vast amounts of gold and gems to level up units, ascend them, and pull for better ones.

Early in the game, when you hit a wall in story progression, the desert map is often the best place to AFK farm for gems. This allows you to accumulate enough resources to strengthen your team and push further. As you advance, consider farming specific Nightmare stages. For instance, Act 1 Nightmare can be highly lucrative for gems, especially if you have units like Whis or Beerus that can one-shot enemies.

Once you've cleared enough story content and reached a certain power level, Tower stages become an excellent source of resources. The Tower can provide valuable units like Sid, who is instrumental in setting up advanced AFK farming methods. The "Sid technique," as it's known, involves exploiting certain map interactions to farm massive amounts of gems and gold overnight. For example, in Hero City, Sid can cause Devil Cyborgs to explode, leading to immense gains in resources. This method is widely known and incredibly effective for accelerating your *Anime Story 2 noob to pro* journey.

Remember to craft essential items like the Dragon Ball relic, which provides a 10% damage boost. While it might seem small, these incremental gains add up and contribute significantly to your overall power.

### Advanced Unit Choices and End-Game Preparation

As you transition from the "noob" phase to "pro," your unit priorities will shift towards more specialized roles and higher rarity units.

After establishing a strong farming foundation, focus on acquiring units like Tenin, especially if you prioritize farming. Tenin is considered a superior farming unit in the early to mid-game. However, Beiru, with his mark and poison abilities, offers more utility in later stages and raids. The choice often depends on your immediate needs and the current banner rotations.

The pursuit of shiny units is also a key aspect of advanced progression. Shiny units offer a 25% stat buff, making them superior to their regular counterparts. While acquiring a shiny unit can be challenging, the ability to "shiny transfer" stats for a gold cost makes it a worthwhile endeavor for your primary units. For example, obtaining a shiny Ace and transferring all your existing Ace's stats to it will drastically improve your main damage dealer.

For events like the slime event, units like Broly are highly effective. Having a shiny Broly, especially with optimized traits, can significantly boost your performance in these limited-time challenges, helping you acquire powerful event-exclusive units like Remaru. Remaru is often considered one of the best units for a Monarch trait due to his universal utility.

### Maximizing Your Gains: Traits, Ascensions, and Beyond

Beyond simply acquiring units, optimizing them through traits and ascensions is crucial for maintaining your edge and solidifying your *Anime Story 2 noob to pro* status.

**Trait Rolling:** Always aim for rainbow traits on your most important units. While some traits like "Dead Eye" might seem less appealing without specific crit gear, any rainbow trait is generally a significant upgrade. Mathematically, a rainbow trait has a 2% chance, meaning it might take around 50 rerolls to obtain one. However, the stat boost is well worth the effort, especially for units you plan to use in raids or challenging content.

**Ascension:** Fully ascending your main damage dealers, such as Ace, to max level (e.g., 300) is a top priority. This unlocks their full AoE potential and ensures they can one-shot most enemies in story mode, allowing for continuous AFK farming. While ascending legendary units requires multiple copies, the efficiency gained from a maxed-out unit far outweighs the initial investment.

**Strategic Unit Management:** Even after acquiring powerful units, consider their specific roles. For instance, a shiny Devil Cyborg might be excellent, but for certain event strategies (like the 9-second strat), a non-shiny, un-maxed version might be preferred to avoid over-leveling. This demonstrates a nuanced understanding of unit utility, a hallmark of an *Anime Story 2 noob to pro* player.
