Embarking on a new adventure in *Anime Story 2* can be both exciting and overwhelming. With countless units, systems, and challenges, knowing where to begin is key to a successful journey. Understanding the core mechanics of *Anime Story 2* is crucial for any aspiring player. The game, at its heart, is an AFK-style stage progression title where you deploy units to defeat waves of enemies across various worlds. Each world features Normal, Hard, and Nightmare difficulties, offering escalating challenges and rewards. To conquer these stages and advance, increasing your power is paramount, and this *Anime Story 2 starter guide* will show you exactly how.

### Assembling Your Dream Team: Early Game Unit Recommendations

Your units are the backbone of your power in *Anime Story 2*. While the gacha system introduces an element of luck, certain units stand out for their early-game utility and long-term potential. When you first dive into the game, after the initial tutorial and your first summon, you'll want to prioritize acquiring specific characters.

For Mythic units, **Broly** is an exceptionally strong choice. His damage output makes him invaluable, especially for tackling the current Rimuru event. Another excellent early-game pick is **Genos**. Although he starts as a secret unit, his evolution into a secret form is very affordable, making him a cost-effective powerhouse. For Legendary units, **Ace** is an underrated gem. While ascending him requires more duplicates, his raw power makes him a worthy investment. Remember, secret and ascended units inherently come at max level (300), bypassing the need for ascension.

### Powering Up Your Units: Stats, Traits, and Ascension

Beyond simply acquiring units, maximizing their potential involves several intricate systems:

*   **Stats:** Units have various stats that can be rerolled using stat chips. "Reroll All" chips randomize all stats, while single chips target individual stats. Takedowns contribute to "worthiness," increasing your chances of rolling higher or more powerful stats.
*   **Shiny Units:** Keep an eye out for shiny variants of units. Shiny units deal more damage, making them highly desirable. You can transfer shininess between units using a "Shiny Transfer" item.
*   **Traits:** Traits provide significant buffs to your units. While obtaining specific rare traits like "Monarch" can be challenging, aiming for "Ethereal" traits on your meta units is a great goal. Farming traits is a core part of optimizing your team.
*   **Ascension:** Ascension is how you break a unit's level cap. By sacrificing duplicate units (or "dupes") of the same character, you can increase their star rating, raising their maximum level. For instance, a two-star unit can reach level 100, and a five-star unit can hit level 300. This process is vital for pushing your units to their peak performance, though secret and ascended units are exempt from this cap.
*   **Evolution:** Some units, like Aizen and Escanor, have evolutions that transform them into more powerful versions. These evolutions often require "stars," which can be farmed through challenges.

### Crafting Relics: Boosting Your Unit's Damage and Survivability

Relics provide crucial stat boosts and unique effects for your units. For any damage-per-second (DPS) unit, the **Baseball Bat** is a must-have, granting a substantial +50% damage bonus. Other relics, like the Elixir, can boost HP.

Crafting relics requires specific shards. The boss stage of each world drops different colored shards. For example, the last boss of the final world drops brown shards, which are essential for crafting the best DPS relic. Regularly farming these boss stages is key to equipping your team with powerful relics.

### Raids, Challenges, and Events: Your Source for Resources

*Anime Story 2* offers various game modes beyond story progression to help you gather essential resources, including gems, coins, stat chips, and more.

*   **Raids:** Currently, there are two main raids: the Bleach raid and the Naruto raid. Act 5 and Act 6 of these raids drop specific units (e.g., Aizen from Bleach, Itachi and Pain from Naruto). Act 6 also offers shiny variants, which are highly sought after for their increased damage. While raids aren't the best for currency, they are crucial for acquiring meta units and their shiny versions. The raid shop, accessible with "Holo Tokens" (from Bleach) and "Ninja Tokens" (from Naruto), allows you to purchase luck potions, traits, evolution items, and even units if your luck is low.
*   **Challenges:** Challenges are your primary source for "stars," which are needed for unit evolutions. Daily challenges provide a good amount, while normal challenges reset every half an hour and offer various currencies and stat chips. Be aware that challenges often come with debuffs, such as enemies having double stats.
*   **Infinite Mode:** Infinite mode is an excellent way to farm rerolls and units. In *Anime Story 1*, it was a top source for rerolls, and it continues to be highly effective in *Anime Story 2*. Forming a team specifically designed to efficiently clear certain waves in Infinite mode can yield great rewards.
*   **Slime Apocalypse Event:** This event, often available during new releases, is a fantastic opportunity to earn "Slime Tokens." These tokens can be used in the summon area to potentially acquire Rimuru, stat cubes, secret orbs, mythic orbs, stat chips, gems, trait tokens, and coins. The event features three boss stages, culminating in a powerful Rimuru boss with a million HP. Broly is particularly effective in this event due to his high damage output.
*   **Tower:** The Tower is a critical game mode for farming. Every 100 floors, you are guaranteed a Sid unit (95% chance for normal, 5% for shiny). According to community tier lists, specific team compositions excel here, often involving two support units and a tank like Pride. The Tower is also an excellent source of rerolls.

### Daily Routines and Essential Shops

To maximize your progress in *Anime Story 2*, establishing a daily routine for resource acquisition is vital.

*   **Coin Shop:** The Coin Shop resets daily and is a prime location to purchase stars, ascension tickets, luck potions, drop potions, and rerolls. Always make sure to clear out these essential items whenever possible.
*   **Index and Level Milestones:** Simply playing the game and discovering new units will allow you to claim gems from your Index. Similarly, reaching level milestones every five levels provides additional gems and traits.
*   **Achievements:** Completing achievements, such as clearing all stages on Normal, Hard, and Nightmare difficulties, rewards you with significant amounts of gems, stat chips, and rerolls. These are often completed naturally as you progress, but it's good to keep track of them.
*   **Battle Pass:** The Battle Pass offers 100 tiers of rewards, including units like Roku (though he's not considered top-tier). A paid version provides shiny unit variants. While the Battle Pass can be a slow grind, *Anime Story 2* is largely an AFK game, so progress will accumulate over time.

### Farming Gold and Gems Efficiently

Gold and gems are crucial for summoning new units and upgrading your existing roster. While there are multiple ways to acquire them, some methods are more efficient than others.

*   **Last World Boss:** According to experienced players, the boss of the last accessible world offers the highest gold and gem rewards per clear. This also provides the brown shards needed for the best DPS relics. Constantly farming this boss, provided your team is strong enough, is an excellent strategy for consistent income.
*   **Rimuru Event and Tower:** As mentioned earlier, the Slime Apocalypse event (if active) can yield a variety of currencies, including gems and coins, through its gacha banner. The Tower also offers gems and opportunities to acquire valuable units that can be sold or used for ascension.

### FAQ

**Q: What are the best units for an *Anime Story 2 starter guide* in early game?**
A: For early game, Broly is an excellent Mythic unit, and Genos is a very cost-effective secret unit that evolves easily. Ace is an underrated Legendary pick.

**Q: How do I increase my unit's level cap in Anime Story 2?**
A: You increase your unit's level cap through "Ascension." By sacrificing duplicate units, you can raise their star rating and, consequently, their maximum level up to 300. Secret and ascended units are already max level.

**Q: What's the most efficient way to farm gold and gems in Anime Story 2?**
A: Many players find that repeatedly clearing the boss stage of the last accessible world offers the best gold and gem rewards. The Slime Apocalypse event (when available) and the Tower also provide good opportunities for currency farming.
