<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Unit Progression and Enhancement">
Beyond acquiring powerful units, consistent progression and enhancement are vital for maintaining the **Anime Story 2 best team**. This involves ascending, evolving, and optimizing unit stats and traits.

### Essential Progression Steps

| Progression Type | Description                                             | How to Achieve                                  | Impact on Unit Power |
| :--------------- | :------------------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------- | :------------------- |
| **Ascension**    | Increases unit's max level cap.                         | Sacrifice duplicate units (up to 5 stars/Lvl 300) | Significantly boosts stats |
| **Evolution**    | Transforms unit into a stronger form, unlocking new abilities.| Requires specific evolution items (e.g., Fairy Spirit, Secret Orbs) | Major power spike, new skills/passives |
| **Stat Rerolls** | Optimizes individual stats (Attack, HP, Defense).       | Use Stat Chips and Reroll All Stat Chips        | Fine-tunes performance |
| **Traits**       | Adds passive bonuses to units.                          | Farmed from raids/challenges, applied via Trait Rolls | Substantial damage/utility buffs |
| **Relics**       | Equippable items providing damage or HP boosts.         | Crafted using shards from world bosses (e.g., Baseball Bat for DPS) | Significant percentage boosts |

<Callout type="tip" title="Farming Relic Shards">
To craft essential relics like the **Baseball Bat** (for +50% damage), focus on defeating the last boss in each world. These bosses drop different colored shards necessary for crafting.
</Callout>
