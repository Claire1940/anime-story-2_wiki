<Section icon={Gem} title="Acquiring and Applying Traits in Anime Story 2">
Obtaining and applying Traits is a straightforward process, primarily relying on **Trait Tokens**. These tokens are your key to rerolling and assigning a random Trait to any unit. Effective management of Trait Tokens is crucial for players looking to fine-tune their roster.

<Callout type="tip" title="Maximizing Trait Acquisition">
Focus on **stage clears** and **daily challenges** to consistently earn Trait Tokens. Don't overlook **code redemptions** as they often provide a quick boost to your token reserves.
</Callout>

Here’s how you can get your hands on Trait Tokens and apply them:

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Earn Trait Tokens">
    Trait Tokens can be acquired through various in-game activities. The most common methods include:
    - **Stage clears**: Successfully completing game stages often rewards you with tokens.
    - **In-game shop purchases**: Tokens can sometimes be bought directly from the shop.
    - **Code redemptions**: Keep an eye out for active codes that grant free tokens.
    - **Daily and Normal Challenges**: Participate in challenges to earn additional tokens.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Navigate to the Upgrades Area">
    Once you have Trait Tokens, head to the "Upgrades" section in the game lobby. Use the "Areas" menu to easily locate this section.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Reroll for Desired Traits">
    Within the Upgrades area, find the "Traits" sub-section. Here, you can spend your Trait Tokens to reroll a random Trait for your selected unit. Continue rerolling until you acquire a Trait that aligns with your unit's role and your strategic needs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="warning" title="Reroll Strategy">
Rerolling is based on chance, so it's wise to save up a good number of **Trait Tokens** before attempting to get the rarer, more powerful Traits. Prioritize rerolling for your most important units first.
</Callout>

</Section>
