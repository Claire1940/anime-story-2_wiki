### Mastering Gem Acquisition: Your Path to Powerful Units

Gems are the lifeblood of *Anime Story*, crucial for summoning new units and advancing your collection. Knowing the most efficient ways to farm them is paramount. Our *Anime Story 2 map guide* highlights several key strategies to keep your gem count high.

The **Desert world on Nightmare mode** stands out as a prime location for gem farming, especially for beginners. With a unit like Beerus, known for his area-of-effect attacks, you can clear the first act in mere seconds. Players report earning approximately 4,500 gems, 15,000 XP, and 473,000 coins within 15 minutes if they consistently clear the stage in under 10 seconds. If you can manage a sub-5-second clear, these rewards effectively double! Beerus doesn't even need to be max level; a level 50 Beerus with a decent mythic tree can achieve these rapid clear times.

Another passive yet effective method for earning gems is through **achievements**. Every world in *Anime Story* offers achievements for completing stages on Normal, Hard, and Nightmare difficulties. Successfully clearing all difficulties for a stage grants you a present filled with various rewards, including gems, rerolls, stat resets, and ascension tickets. These accumulate naturally as you play, so make sure to claim them regularly.

Finally, the **Tower** offers a substantial gem income, particularly for players with strong units. Each floor you clear awards 500 gems. While it can be challenging, even clearing a few floors can significantly boost your gem reserves. Many players leverage the Desert Nightmare strategy until they acquire their first powerful mythic unit, then transition to the Tower for consistent gem gains.

### Rerolls: Sculpting Your Ultimate Squad

Rerolls are essential for optimizing your units' traits, turning good units into exceptional ones. This part of our *Anime Story 2 map guide* will detail the most effective ways to stock up on these valuable items.

The **Tower** isn't just for gems; it's also a fantastic source of rerolls. For every five floors you clear, you'll receive five rerolls. This makes the Tower a dual-purpose farming location, rewarding both your combat prowess and strategic unit development.

**Raids** also offer a chance at rerolls, though it's less consistent. The last act of raids provides a 1% chance for free rerolls. While seemingly low, fast clear times increase your chances of accumulating a good number of these. Additionally, the **shop** allows you to purchase 20 trait rerolls daily, which restocks every 24 hours. This provides a reliable, albeit costly, source of rerolls.

For those with specific units, a community-favorite strategy involves **Monarch Madera** and the **Straw Hat relic**. By equipping Monarch Madera with the Straw Hat and pairing her with any level one unit to survive, you can head to **Realm 7 of the Old Infinite**. Realm 7 offers a 10% chance for 2-5 rerolls upon reaching wave five. This method is incredibly efficient, taking only about 30 seconds per attempt, with some players reporting 600-800 rerolls daily. This specialized technique truly solidifies your reroll farming capabilities within the *Anime Story 2 map guide*.

### Accumulating Coins: Fueling Your Unit Upgrades

Coins are fundamental for leveling up your units, a process that can become quite expensive as you progress. Our *Anime Story 2 map guide* ensures you're never short on gold.

Similar to gems, the **Nightmare Desert** is an excellent early-game spot for coin farming. If you have Beerus, you can achieve sub-5-second clears, yielding significant coin rewards. This dual benefit makes the Desert an indispensable farming location for new players.

For more advanced players with evolved mythics, secrets, and units like Monarch or Ethereals, **Realm 7 in Nightmare mode** becomes the go-to for coins. A single clear can net you 90,000 coins. With powerful units, you can one-shot enemies, making this a rapid and highly rewarding farming method. While you'll passively gain coins just by playing the game and AFK raiding, actively grinding these specific locations will ensure a steady influx of gold.
