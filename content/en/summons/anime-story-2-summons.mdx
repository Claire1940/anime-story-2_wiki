### Understanding the Summoning Economy: Gems, Gold, and Rerolls

Before you dive headfirst into the summoning pool, it's essential to grasp the core currencies and systems that drive the gacha in *Anime Story 2*. Your primary resources for summoning are **Gems** and **Gold**, with **Rerolls** playing a significant role in optimizing your units.

*   **Gems:** These are the premium currency, primarily used for summoning on banners that feature Mythic and Secret units. You can acquire gems through various in-game activities, including story progression, quests, and AFK farming. Early game, the desert map is a popular spot for gem farming, and later, specific Nightmare stages like the Sid farming method can yield millions of gems overnight.
*   **Gold:** While not directly used for summoning new units from banners, gold is vital for leveling up and ascending your characters. Maxing out a unit can cost a significant amount of gold (around 28 million for a single unit), making efficient gold farming equally as important as gem farming.
*   **Rerolls:** These are crucial for optimizing your units' traits. A good trait can significantly boost a unit's performance, especially for key damage dealers like Ace or utility units like Beiru. Codes often provide a generous amount of rerolls, ensuring you have a good start.

Initial resources from codes can be substantial. For instance, a new account might start with over 100,000 gems, 29,000 gold, and 1,300 rerolls, plus a guaranteed secret orb. This head start is invaluable for getting your first strong units and kickstarting your *Anime Story 2 summons* journey.

*   **Gojo:** Excellent for damage over time (DOT) and general combat.

The strategy for early game is often to get a strong AoE unit like Ace and then complement him with units that provide utility or additional damage. For example, Beiru, with his mark and poison application, becomes more useful in the mid to late game, but Tenin is often preferred for early farming due to his efficiency.

### Advanced Summoning Strategies: Secrets, Shinies, and Pity Systems

Once you have a solid foundation, you can start focusing on more advanced summoning goals: acquiring Secret units, Shiny variants, and leveraging the pity system.

**Secret Units:**
Secret units are among the rarest and most powerful characters in *Anime Story 2*. They often provide unique abilities that can completely change your team composition or farming efficiency.

*   **Secret Orbs:** These guarantee a Secret unit. Codes can sometimes provide these, giving new players an immediate boost.
*   **Targeting Specific Secrets:** Banners will often feature specific Secret units with increased rates. For example, if Tenin is on an 80% banner, it's a prime opportunity to try and pull him, especially if you need a strong farming unit. Conversely, if Beiru is the 80% unit, and you already have him, you might hold off for a more desirable Secret.

**Shiny Units:**
Shiny units offer a 25% buff to their stats, making them highly sought after. While they don't change a unit's core abilities, the stat boost can be significant.

*   **Shiny Hunter Game Pass:** This game pass is considered essential by serious players, as it increases your chances of finding shiny units.
*   **Shiny Transfer:** If you pull a shiny version of a unit you've already invested in, you can transfer the shiny status (and its associated buffs) to your existing unit. This process costs a substantial amount of gold (e.g., 50 million), but it's a worthwhile investment for maximizing your team's power.
*   **Luck Potions:** These temporary buffs increase your chances of getting rare pulls, including shinies. Use them strategically on banners where you're targeting specific high-value units.

**Pity System:**
*Anime Story 2* incorporates a pity system, ensuring that after a certain number of unsuccessful *Anime Story 2 summons*, you are guaranteed to receive a rare unit. This system is crucial for planning your gem spending, as it prevents endless bad luck streaks. Knowing the pity count allows you to save up gems for specific banners and guarantee key pulls. For instance, accumulating millions of gems can ensure you hit pity for a desired Secret unit.

### Optimizing Your Units: Traits and Ascension

Pulling powerful units is only half the battle; optimizing them with the right traits and ascending them is equally important for maximizing their potential.

*   **Traits:** Every unit can have a trait, which provides various bonuses. For damage dealers, traits like "Berserk" are highly desirable for early-game boosts. For utility units or tanks, traits that enhance their specific roles are preferred. Rainbow traits are the rarest and most powerful, offering significant buffs. Rerolling traits can be expensive, but it's a necessary step for end-game optimization. Players often aim for "Monarch" traits on top-tier units like Remaru or Broly, as these are universally strong.
*   **Ascension:** Ascending units requires multiple copies of the same character. For legendary units like Ace, you'll need several duplicates to reach max ascension. Ascension significantly boosts a unit's base stats and can unlock new abilities or enhance existing ones. This is why farming for duplicates of key legendary units is so important.

### Farming Strategies for Gems and Gold

Consistent farming is the backbone of successful *Anime Story 2 summons*. Without a steady influx of gems and gold, you won't be able to keep up with the demands of new banners and unit upgrades.

*   **Early Game Farming:**
    *   **Desert Map (AFK):** The desert map is often cited as the best early-game location for AFK gem farming.
    *   **Nightmare Act 1 (Whis/Beerus):** These units offer 3 AoE and can be efficient for farming if your Ace isn't strong enough yet.
    *   **Story Progression:** Completing story stages and quests provides a good initial burst of gems and gold.
*   **Mid-to-Late Game Farming:**
    *   **Sid Farming Method (Hero City Nightmare):** Once you acquire Sid, a powerful farming strategy involves exploiting a bug with Devil Cyborgs in Hero City (Nightmare difficulty). This method can yield massive amounts of gems (e.g., 285 gems per run) and gold (500k coins) overnight. This bug allows Sid to cause Devil Cyborgs to explode, making it an incredibly efficient AFK farm.
    *   **Raid Farming:** Raids can be a good source of resources, especially for specific event items or rare drops.
    *   **Slime Event:** Events like the Slime Event are crucial for acquiring limited-time units like Remaru, often requiring specific teams and efficient farming.
