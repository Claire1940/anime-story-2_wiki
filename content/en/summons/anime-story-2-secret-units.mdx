## Understanding the Rarity: What Makes Secret Units Special?

In the world of Anime Story 2, power isn't just about the color of your unit's border; it's about the ceiling of their potential. While Mythic units are the standard for high-level play, Secret and Ascended units take things a step further. One of the most significant advantages of these elite characters is their level cap. Unlike standard units that require constant "Ascension" (sacrificing duplicates to raise the level cap from 50 up to 300), many secret units come with an inherent level cap of 300 right out of the box.

According to veteran players, the sheer utility of a secret unit often lies in its passive abilities. For example, the secret version of Itachi boasts a 25% chance to avoid all incoming damage and a 50% counter-attack rate. This makes him not just a damage dealer, but a defensive anchor for your team. When you combine these stats with the right traits and relics, **anime story 2 secret units** become virtually unstoppable in both Raids and the Infinite Tower.

## How to Obtain Anime Story 2 Secret Units

There are currently three primary ways to add these elusive characters to your roster. Each method requires a different level of commitment, ranging from pure luck on the summon banners to dedicated grinding in the game's most challenging modes.

### 1. The Summoning Banner
The most direct, albeit luck-dependent, way to find **anime story 2 secret units** is through the main summoning banner. When you view the rates on the banner, you will see a tiny percentage chance for secret units alongside the Mythics and Legendaries. While the odds are low, players can increase their chances by using Luck Potions, which are available in the Raid Shop or through daily rewards.

### 2. The Infinite Tower (Sid Farming)
The Infinite Tower is arguably the most consistent way to secure a secret unit. Every 100 floors you clear, you are guaranteed a drop for the unit "Sid." 
*   **Normal Sid:** There is a 95% chance to receive the standard version.
*   **Shiny Sid:** There is a 5% chance to receive the Shiny variant, which deals significantly more damage.
Since the Tower has no upper limit, dedicated players farm these 100-floor milestones repeatedly to "dupe" their Sid or hunt for that elusive Shiny version.

### 3. Evolution Paths (The Genos Method)
Some units don't start as secrets but can be evolved into them. Genos is a prime example of a unit that is relatively "cheap" to evolve early in the game but eventually reaches secret-tier power. This makes him a top recommendation for beginners who haven't had luck with the gacha system yet. By collecting specific evolution items and stars from challenges, you can transform a standard Genos into a meta-defining secret powerhouse.

## Spotlight on the Meta: Best Secret Units to Use

If you are lucky enough to pull or grind for multiple high-tier characters, you need to know which ones deserve your resources. Based on community feedback and recent tier lists, these are the standout **anime story 2 secret units** you should prioritize:

*   **Sid (The Tower King):** As a guaranteed drop every 100 floors, Sid is the backbone of most F2P (Free to Play) teams. His damage scaling is excellent for clearing waves in Infinite mode.
*   **Itachi (The Counter Master):** With his ability to dodge and counter-attack, Itachi is a "must-have" for high-level Raids where bosses can one-shot your squishier units.

*   **Aizen (The Raid Specialist):** Obtained through the Bleach Raid (Act 5 and 6), Aizen is widely considered a meta-defining unit due to his high DPS and synergy with other Bleach-themed characters.

## Maximizing Power: Traits, Stats, and Relics

Simply owning **anime story 2 secret units** isn't enough to reach the top of the leaderboards. You must optimize them through the game’s various upgrade systems.

### Rolling for the Best Traits
Traits provide massive percentage-based buffs to your units. Currently, the community consensus is that **Ethereal** and **Monarch** are the two best traits in the game. Ethereal is more common but provides a substantial boost to damage and speed, while Monarch is the rarest and most powerful. If you are farming rerolls, the Infinite mode and the Tower are your best sources for trait tokens.

### Stat Chips and Worthiness
Every unit has individual stats that can be rerolled using Stat Chips. By participating in takedowns and challenges, you earn "Worthiness." High worthiness increases your chance of rolling "Powerful" or "Godly" stats on your secret units. 
*   **Pro Tip:** Always use "Reroll All" chips if your unit has poor stats across the board, but save "Single Reroll" chips for when you are trying to perfect that last 1% of a specific stat like Crit Damage.

### Crafting the Perfect Relics
Relics are equippable items that provide unique bonuses. For any DPS-focused secret unit, the **Baseball Bat** is currently the gold standard. It provides a massive +50% damage boost. To craft it, you’ll need to farm the last boss of the final world to obtain Brown Shards. For tankier units like Pride (Escanor), the **Elixir** is a better choice as it significantly buffs HP.

## Farming Strategies for Secret Evolution Items

To evolve units like Genos or Aizen, you will need a steady supply of Stars and specialized items. These aren't found in the normal story worlds but require participation in timed events.

1.  **Challenges:** These reset every 30 minutes and are the primary way to earn Stars. Be aware that challenges often come with "debuffs," such as enemies having double stats. Ensure your team has a dedicated tank and at least two support units (like Gojo or Rimuru) to survive these stages.
2.  **The Raid Shop:** By clearing the Bleach or Naruto raids, you earn Holo and Ninja tokens. These can be spent in the Raid Shop on evolution items that are otherwise extremely rare. The shop resets daily, so check back often for "Evil Items" required for top-tier evolutions.
3.  **The Coin Shop:** If you have an excess of gold from AFK farming the final world, you can occasionally buy Stars and Ascension tickets directly from the Coin Shop.

## The Slime Apocalypse: Event-Exclusive Secrets

During special events like the Slime Apocalypse, players have the chance to obtain unique **anime story 2 secret units** like Rimuru. These events function differently than standard raids. In the Slime Apocalypse, your units do not heal between the three boss stages. You must defeat a million-HP Rimuru at the end to earn Slime Tokens.

These tokens can be used on a special event banner. Not only does this banner offer a chance at the Rimuru unit, but it also drops Stat Cubes, Secret Orbs, and Mythic Orbs. For many players, AFK farming this event is the most efficient way to stockpile resources for their existing secret units while trying to pull a new one.

## Community Tips for Success

Success in Anime Story 2 often comes down to efficiency. Here are a few "pro" tips gathered from the [Official Roblox Page](https://www.roblox.com/games/10452332152/Anime-Story-2) and community Discord servers:
*   **Shiny Transfer:** If you pull a Shiny version of a lower-tier unit, don't delete it! You can transfer the Shiny status to your Secret units later, which is a massive damage multiplier.
*   **The "Two-Support" Rule:** For Tower pushing, always run at least two support units. Support characters like Gojo provide crowd control or buffs that allow your secret DPS units to survive longer.
*   **Achievement Hunting:** Don't ignore the achievements menu. Completing all stages on Nightmare difficulty provides a huge influx of Gems, which are vital for hitting the "pity" counter on secret banners.

### FAQ: Anime Story 2 Secret Units

**Q: What is the fastest way to get a secret unit as a beginner?**
A: The fastest way is to evolve Genos. He is a high-tier unit that is relatively easy to evolve compared to other **anime story 2 secret units**. Alternatively, grinding the first 100 floors of the Tower for a guaranteed Sid is a great way to start.

**Q: How often do secret units drop from the standard banner?**
A: The drop rates for secret units are typically below 0.1%. It is highly recommended to use Luck Potions and save your Gems until you have enough to potentially hit a pity milestone if one is active.

**Q: Is a Shiny Mythic better than a normal Secret unit?**
A: It depends on the unit. Generally, a Secret unit has a higher level cap (300) and better passives. However, a Shiny Mythic with a "Monarch" trait can often out-damage a standard Secret unit if the Secret unit has poor traits.

**Q: Can I farm secret units while AFK?**
A: Yes. Many players AFK farm the "Slime Apocalypse" event or the Infinite mode to collect tokens and gems, which are then used to pull for **anime story 2 secret units** on the banners.
