<Section icon={Target} title="Secret Boss Locations and Strategies">

To obtain the materials for **anime story 2 yuno**, you must venture outside the tower and defeat the Secret Bosses scattered across different maps. Each boss has a unique gimmick and a low drop rate for shiny versions.

### Yami (Black Kingdom)
Located in the Black Kingdom, Yami is notorious for his "Undying" passive. He will survive with 1% HP regardless of how much damage you deal in a single turn.
- **Strategy**: Use Beerus for poison damage or Gojo to stall. You must end the turn to finish him off once he hits the 1% threshold.
- **Kill Time**: Approximately 30 seconds.

### Julius (Titan City)
Julius is a Support/Light class boss. While he is getting a buff in future updates, he currently serves as a hurdle for those seeking the Asta Sword quest items.
- **Strategy**: Aizen is the MVP here. A single crit from Aizen can remove half of Julius's HP.
- **Kill Time**: 15-20 seconds with a high-DPS team.

### Luck (Gold District)
Luck's primary gimmick is his speed. He has a passive that ensures he always attacks first in every round.
- **Strategy**: Use Beerus to apply a 20% damage vulnerability debuff. Follow up with Anking's poison for a guaranteed kill.
- **Kill Time**: 23 seconds.
