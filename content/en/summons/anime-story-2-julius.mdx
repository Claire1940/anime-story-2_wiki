<Section icon={Target} title="Update 0.5 Progression: The Black Clover Questline">

Julius is more than just a powerful unit; he is a key progression gate for the entire Black Clover update. To obtain the powerful Asta Swords required for Asta's evolution, players must complete a tedious but rewarding questline that involves almost every new unit in the update.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Obtain Secret Units">
    Farm the four secret bosses: Julius, Yami, Lock, and the 23rd Magic Emperor. You will need these units in your inventory to progress the quest stages.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Nature Tower Grind">
    Reach floor 333 in the Nature Tower to obtain the 10 Orbs required for Julius's evolution. If you aim for Yuno, you must push further to floor 500.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Ascend the Emperor">
    Use the Orbs and gold to evolve Julius into his Ascended King form. This is a mandatory step for the Asta Sword questline.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Complete the Questline">
    Once Julius is ascended and other unit requirements are met, finish the questline to receive 75 rerolls, 25 secret orbs, and the legendary Asta Swords.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Swords} title="Farming Optimization: Best Teams for the Time Emperor Boss">

Efficiency is key when dealing with a 2.5% drop rate. To maximize your chances of getting **anime story 2 julius**, you need a team that can clear the Titan City secret boss in under 20 seconds. Because Julius is a Light element boss, Dark units receive a significant damage multiplier.

| Recommended Unit | Role | Reason for Inclusion |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Aizen** | Main DPS | Dark element deals 2x damage; 250% damage passive at start. |
| **Tempest** | Support | Applies Black Flames for 66% damage that cannot be extinguished. |
| **Mereoleona** | Leader | Provides 10% damage buff to Brawler allies in slot one. |
| **Cyborg** | Buffer | Provides Light-based buffs that synergize with high-stat attackers. |

**Farming Strategy:**
- **Prioritize Dark Damage**: Aizen is the MVP for this fight. His initial burst damage can bypass Julius's health phases quickly.
- **Animation Toggling**: Turn off move animations in the settings to reduce the time spent per run.
- **Shiny Hunting**: If you are specifically looking for the Shiny version, ensure you have the Shiny Hunter blessing active if available.

<Callout type="warning" title="Inventory Management">
The 0.5 update added a filter for Shiny Mythics in the banner. Ensure your inventory is cleared of useless common and rare units before a long farming session to avoid losing secret drops due to a full inventory.
