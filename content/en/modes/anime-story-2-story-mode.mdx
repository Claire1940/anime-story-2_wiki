### Crushing the Early Game: Your First Steps to Victory

The initial stages of *Anime Story 2 story mode* set the foundation for your entire playthrough. Making smart choices early on can save you hours of grinding and frustration. Focus on building a versatile team capable of handling various enemy types and scenarios.

A key strategy for early campaign success lies in utilizing units with Area of Effect (AoE) abilities. These characters can clear multiple enemies at once, drastically speeding up your progression through the normal stages. Consider units like Time Angel, whose initial attack targets three units, or Ethereal Archer, who hits two. Even basic versions of these units can make a significant difference.

Another crucial early-game objective is acquiring Mythic Voga. This powerful unit is surprisingly easy to obtain and can carry you through the majority of the normal story. To get him, you simply need to summon the Epic Vegeta unit, then evolve him using 500 green shards dropped by the World 1 boss. While 500 shards may sound like a lot, the World 1 boss can be farmed quickly, often in under 10 seconds per run, making this a very efficient upgrade. Mythic Voga's passive, which grants 50% more damage every turn, combined with AoE units, creates a formidable early-game team.

### Building Your Dream Team: Top Units for Campaign Progression

As you delve deeper into *Anime Story 2 story mode*, your unit composition becomes increasingly vital. While many units can contribute, some stand out for their exceptional utility and raw power.

*   **Mythic Voga:** As mentioned, he's an absolute powerhouse for the campaign. His damage scaling makes him a consistent threat, especially when paired with units that can clear out weaker foes.
*   **Time Angel:** Beyond his healing capabilities, his AoE attack is invaluable for clearing waves. A shiny version offers a slight but noticeable boost to stats, making him even more effective.
*   **Ethereal Archer:** Another excellent AoE option, providing good crowd control and potentially setting up critical damage for your other units if you invest in crit chance.
*   **Reaper:** While his multi-target ability can be a bit RNG-dependent, a well-placed attack can clear a significant portion of the enemy wave. Evolving him further enhances his potential.
*   **Eizen:** Widely considered one of the best characters in the game, Eizen is a top-tier pick for any content, including *Anime Story 2 story mode*. His ability to stun enemies for a turn is incredibly broken in a turn-based battler, giving your team a massive advantage. Farming the Eizen raid should be a high priority once you're capable, as it provides everything from trait rerolls and coins to gems and Eizen himself.
*   **Jin Wu:** If you find yourself struggling with boss encounters, Jin Wu is your go-to. His passive grants 100% more damage to bosses, making him an indispensable asset for taking down tough adversaries.

### Understanding Traits and Evolutions

Traits play a significant role in optimizing your units. While normal traits offer a baseline boost, shiny versions provide a percentage boost on top, making them highly desirable for your core team members. Always aim to get shiny versions of your most-used characters if possible.

Evolution is another critical aspect of character progression. Many units transform into more powerful versions by acquiring specific materials. It's important to note that while some evolution requirements might list "takedowns" (e.g., 1,000 kills), players have reported that these kill requirements are often unnecessary. Focus primarily on gathering the required materials.

For example, evolving the Epic Vegeta into Mythic Voga only requires the green shards, not a specific number of kills. Similarly, evolving other units like a specific Mythic into its Secret form (as seen with a unit obtained from the slime banner) primarily relies on collecting special orbs.

### Mid-Game Optimization: Raids and Resources

Once you've established a solid team and are comfortably progressing through the *Anime Story 2 story mode*, your focus should shift towards mid-game optimization. This primarily involves farming raids and managing your resources effectively.

The **Eizen raid** is arguably the most important mid-game activity. It’s a treasure trove of essential resources, including:

*   **Coins and XP:** For leveling up your units and abilities.
*   **Gems:** Premium currency for summons and other purchases.
*   **Hollow Tokens:** Specific currency for certain items.
*   **Trait Rerolls:** Crucial for perfecting your unit’s stats.
*   **Eizen Unit:** As discussed, a top-tier character that will significantly boost your team's power.

Farming the Eizen raid consistently will provide everything you need to strengthen your roster, making subsequent campaign stages and endgame content much more manageable.

Don't forget to check the **Index** for level rewards and **Summons** regularly. Pulling on banners whenever possible can yield new powerful units or duplicates for ascension. The **Tower** is another excellent source of gold and diamonds, and reportedly offers a chance at a shiny unit every 100 floors, making it a worthwhile long-term goal.

### Advanced Strategies for Challenging Stages

Even with a strong team, some stages in *Anime Story 2 story mode* can prove particularly difficult. Here are some advanced tips to overcome these hurdles:

*   **Team Composition Flexibility:** Don't be afraid to experiment with your team. While a core group is essential, swapping in situational units like Jin Wu for boss stages or a unit with unique utility (like a character that nukes upon death) can turn the tide.
*   **Stat Rolling:** Once you have a good unit, invest in rolling their stats. For example, if you have Ethereal Archer, prioritizing crit damage rolls can amplify his effectiveness and benefit other units with passive crit damage buffs.
*   **Shiny Units:** The slight statistical advantage from shiny units can be the difference in close battles. Prioritize obtaining shiny versions of your most important characters.
*   **Understanding Unit Synergies:** Some units work exceptionally well together. For instance, a unit that boosts crit damage can synergize perfectly with characters that have high crit rates or abilities that trigger critical hits.
*   **Don't Rush Evolutions:** While evolutions are powerful, ensure you have the materials ready. There's no benefit to holding onto a unit for a "takedown" requirement that might not even be necessary.

### Frequently Asked Questions about Anime Story 2 Story Mode

**Q: What are the best units for early game in Anime Story 2 story mode?**
A: For the early game, focus on obtaining Mythic Voga by evolving Epic Vegeta, and utilize units with strong Area of Effect (AoE) abilities like Time Angel and Ethereal Archer to clear stages quickly.

**Q: How do I get Mythic Voga for the campaign?**
A: To get Mythic Voga, you need to summon the Epic Vegeta unit, then farm 500 green shards from the World 1 boss. Once you have the shards, you can evolve Epic Vegeta into his Mythic form.

**Q: Is Eizen good for Anime Story 2 story mode?**
A: Yes, Eizen is considered one of the best units in the game, and his stun ability is incredibly powerful for both story mode and other content. Farming the Eizen raid should be a priority once your team is strong enough.

**Q: Do I need to get kills for unit evolutions in Anime Story 2?**
A: According to player experience, the "takedown" or "kill" requirements listed for some unit evolutions are often not necessary. Focus on gathering the required material items for evolution.

**Q: What should I prioritize farming in the mid-game for Anime Story 2 story mode progression?**
A: The Eizen raid is the most crucial mid-game farm. It provides coins, XP, gems, trait rerolls, and the powerful Eizen unit, all of which are essential for advancing through the story and preparing for endgame.
