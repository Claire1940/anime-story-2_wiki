### Essential Units for Tower Domination

To consistently progress in the *Anime Story 2 Tower*, certain units are considered near-mandatory due to their unique abilities and impact on the battlefield. These units form the backbone of many successful teams:

*   **Eizen:** Eizen is an absolute must-have. His ability to stun three enemies at the start of a round for one turn is game-changing. Furthermore, his 200% damage bonus on the first attack allows him to often one-shot early threats. This combination of crowd control and burst damage makes him indispensable for managing enemy waves and preventing them from overwhelming your defenses.
*   **Itachi:** Another critical unit, Itachi boasts a 25% chance to avoid damage and counterattack for 50% damage when hit. Crucially, after being hit, his next incoming attack misses. This passive ability introduces a significant layer of RNG, but when it procs, it can single-handedly turn the tide of a battle by negating dangerous attacks and providing consistent damage.
*   **Slime:** Often underestimated, Slime is a low-key must-have for the *Anime Story 2 Tower*. He provides a crucial 10% damage buff to your team and his "black flames" ability can deal 66% damage, which can be exchanged to make enemies take 10% more damage. This dual utility of buffing allies and debuffing enemies makes him a valuable asset for maximizing your team's overall output.

While these three form a strong core, other units can complement them depending on the specific floor and enemy composition.

### Strategic Unit Considerations and RNG Management

The *Anime Story 2 Tower* is heavily influenced by RNG, meaning even the best team can fall victim to unlucky rolls. However, strategic unit selection can help mitigate some of this randomness.

*   **Cyborg:** Cyborg is a controversial pick. Upon death, he detonates, dealing massive damage to all enemies. The trick is to have him at a specific level (around 193) where he has just enough health to die quickly and unleash his explosion, clearing AoE threats. If he's too high level, he won't die fast enough, disrupting your strategy. He excels against enemy teams with multiple AoE units, but if your entire team is wiped out, it's still a loss.
*   **Sound Breather:** While seemingly suicidal, Sound Breather can decimate enemy teams if his combo lands. This is another high-risk, high-reward unit that leans into the RNG aspect of the *Anime Story 2 Tower*.
*   **Beiru:** Often paired with units, Beiru can significantly enhance damage output. Equipping him with a baseball bat (or similar damage-boosting relic) can maximize his effectiveness, especially when combined with high-DPS units.
*   **Igoras:** This unit can be incredibly powerful once he starts "rolling" (gaining momentum or buffs). However, facing an enemy Igoras can be devastating, as he can quickly snowball and wipe out your team.

When constructing your team, always consider the enemy lineup. If you face an enemy Itachi, his passive will apply, making him incredibly difficult to hit. Similarly, an enemy Eizen can stun your key units, disrupting your carefully planned attacks. This constant adaptation is what makes the *Anime Story 2 Tower* so engaging yet challenging.

### Optimizing Your Tower Team Composition

Building the optimal team for the *Anime Story 2 Tower* involves more than just picking strong units; it's about creating synergy and adapting to enemy compositions.

1.  **Core Stun & Damage:** Eizen is almost always your first pick. His stun and burst damage are invaluable.
2.  **Defensive RNG:** Itachi provides a crucial layer of defense and counter-attack potential, especially against high-damage single-target threats.
3.  **Support & Debuff:** Slime's team-wide buffs and enemy debuffs can significantly boost your overall damage and survivability.
4.  **Flexible Slot(s):** The remaining slots are where you adapt.
    *   **High DPS:** Units like Tempest can provide excellent raw damage, especially against multiple targets.
    *   **Specialized Counters:** If facing many AoE enemies, a correctly leveled Cyborg can be a game-changer.
    *   **Utility:** Beiru can boost damage, while other units might offer specific crowd control or healing.

Remember to constantly adjust your team based on the specific floor you're attempting. There's no single "best" team for every scenario in the *Anime Story 2 Tower*; flexibility is key. For example, a stage with double Eizen and Igoras requires a very different approach than one with multiple AoE units. Player experience suggests that having a good mix of stun, damage, and defensive capabilities, along with some RNG mitigation, is the most consistent path to success.

### Relics and Enhancements

Relics play a crucial role in empowering your units for the *Anime Story 2 Tower*. The baseball bat, for instance, is a popular choice for increasing a unit's HP, making them more resilient to incoming damage and potentially allowing them to proc their abilities more often. For Itachi, giving him the most HP ensures he can take hits and utilize his counter-attack passive effectively.

Ensuring your units have the correct stats and enhancements is vital. A unit with triple S stats in attack, crit range, or crit damage, depending on their role, will perform significantly better. While relics can boost these stats, careful consideration of traits and how they interact with relics is also important. For instance, the "Dead Eye" trait could synergize well with a crit-focused relic.
