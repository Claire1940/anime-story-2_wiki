## How to Optimize Your Unit Synergy with Traits

Simply having a high-damage trait isn't always enough; you must pair it with the right unit. For example, a "Speed" trait might actually provide more total DPS on a unit with a very long wind-up time than a pure damage trait would. However, for most "glass cannon" units, focusing purely on **anime story 2 damage traits** is the recommended path.

Consider the following synergy tips:
*   **Multi-Hit Units**: Benefit most from traits that have a "chance to proc" extra damage on every hit.
*   **AOE Units**: Should focus on traits that increase damage based on the number of enemies hit.
*   **Boss Killers**: Require the highest possible single-target multiplier, such as the "Slayer" or "Godly" traits.

By matching the trait to the unit's natural kit, you can maximize the efficiency of every stat point. This is often referred to as "build harmony" by the community's top theorists.

A common mistake among intermediate players is choosing utility traits—like those that increase gold gain or experience—over pure damage. While these utility traits are helpful for "farm units," your primary combat team should always prioritize **anime story 2 damage traits**.

In the endgame, the "DPS check" is the most common reason for failure. If you cannot kill a boss before it triggers its ultimate ability, no amount of extra gold or movement speed will save the run. Therefore, the general rule of thumb is:
1.  Roll for Damage on your main attackers.
2.  Roll for Cooldown Reduction on your supports.
