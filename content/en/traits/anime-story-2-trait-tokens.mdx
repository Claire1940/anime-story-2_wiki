### Daily Quests and Challenges: Your Consistent Token Stream

One of the most straightforward and reliable ways to obtain trait tokens in *Anime Story 2* is through daily activities. These tasks are designed to be completed regularly, offering a consistent trickle of tokens that quickly add up.

*   **Daily Quests:** Make it a habit to complete your daily quests. These typically offer a small but steady amount of trait rerolls. While individual rewards might seem modest, their cumulative effect over time is significant.
*   **30-Minute Challenges:** Keep an eye on the 30-minute challenges. These timed events often reward a decent number of trait rerolls upon completion. Integrating these into your routine can provide a healthy boost to your token reserves.
*   **Weekly Quests:** Don't overlook the weekly quests! These usually demand a bit more effort but compensate with a larger payout of trait tokens, often around 15 per week. Prioritizing these can significantly impact your weekly reroll count.

By diligently tackling these daily and weekly tasks, players can easily accumulate a good foundation of trait tokens without intense grinding. This approach is particularly beneficial for new players establishing their unit rosters.

### Raids: The Powerhouse for Trait Token Farming

For dedicated players, Raids stand out as one of the most lucrative sources for **Anime Story 2 Trait Tokens**. Not only do they offer substantial token rewards, but they also provide other valuable resources like gold and experience.

*   **Daily Raid Rewards:** Participating in raids daily is highly recommended. Many raids offer trait tokens as part of their completion rewards. For instance, specific raids can grant 25 trait rerolls, and with multiple raid options, this can quickly add up to 75 or more tokens daily.
*   **Shiny Unit Farming:** An often-overlooked benefit of raid farming is the chance for trait rerolls to drop when farming for shiny units. Certain raids offer a 1% chance to drop two trait rerolls per run, which can be completed very quickly (sometimes in just 3 seconds). This makes raid farming a dual-purpose activity, netting you shiny units and valuable rerolls simultaneously.

The key to efficient raid farming is having a strong, Area-of-Effect (AoE) unit. For early-game players, Ace (especially if shiny and leveled) is an excellent choice for soloing easier nightmare story maps to farm gems, which can then be used for summons that yield stat chips. For end-game players, units like Rimuru (Tempest) are preferred due to their full AoE capabilities and survivability, making them ideal for high-tier raids and story maps like Hero City Nightmare, which offers impressive rewards for gems, coins, and XP.

### Tower Resets: A Hidden Gem for Trait Rerolls

The Tower in *Anime Story 2* is a fantastic, often cyclical source of trait rerolls that many players might not fully utilize.

*   **Floor Rewards:** The Tower rewards players with trait rerolls every five floors. This means that for every floor you clear, you're essentially earning one trait reroll. When the Tower resets, you can climb it again, accumulating a significant number of tokens.
*   **Cumulative Gains:** Players have reported accumulating hundreds of trait rerolls from Tower resets alone. This makes the Tower an invaluable resource for consistent token farming, especially when combined with other methods.

Regularly checking the Tower's reset schedule and making a concerted effort to climb as high as possible each cycle can provide a massive influx of **Anime Story 2 Trait Tokens**. This method also yields gold and gems, making it a multi-purpose farming activity.

### Infinite Mode and Summoning: Indirect Token Gains

While not direct sources, Infinite Mode and the summoning system can indirectly contribute to your supply of trait tokens.

*   **Infinite Mode:** Infinite Mode offers gems for every stage completed. While these gems aren't directly trait tokens, they are crucial for summoning units.
*   **Summoning Rewards:** When you perform numerous summons on banners, you accumulate stat chips. Although the video reference doesn't explicitly state that stat chips directly convert to trait tokens, the overall process of active play, summoning, and engaging with events often ties into the broader economy where resources can be exchanged or used to strengthen units, reducing the need for extensive rerolls on weaker characters. The more units you summon, the more likely you are to get strong units, potentially saving you rerolls on existing ones.
*   **Optimal Reroll Strategy in Infinite:** For new players, the optimal strategy in Infinite Mode involves reaching stage 5, then intentionally letting your team die. This allows you to quickly repeat runs for a 2% chance at a unit drop (which used to be 1%). While not directly tokens, acquiring more units means more opportunities to find units with desirable traits, or units that you can use to upgrade others, indirectly impacting your need for rerolls.

### Shop and Battle Pass: Direct Purchases and Premium Rewards

For those looking for more direct acquisition or willing to invest, the Coin Shop and Battle Pass offer immediate access to trait tokens.

*   **Coin Shop:** The Coin Shop frequently offers trait rerolls for purchase using in-game currency. Keeping an eye on the shop's inventory and making strategic purchases can supplement your farming efforts.
*   **Battle Pass:** The Battle Pass is an excellent investment, especially for early-game players. It provides a plethora of rewards, including a substantial amount of gems and **Anime Story 2 Trait Tokens**, as you progress through its tiers. The value offered often outweighs the cost, making it a worthwhile consideration.

While these methods might require spending in-game currency or real money, they provide a guaranteed and often significant boost to your trait token reserves, allowing for more immediate unit optimization.
