## Essential Free Methods for Trait Rerolls

Before diving into more intensive farming, several free and straightforward methods can provide you with a steady trickle of trait reroll tokens. While individually these might not yield massive amounts, consistently utilizing them will add up over time.

*   **Redeem Codes:** Always check for active in-game codes! Developers frequently release codes that offer a variety of rewards, often including a substantial number of trait reroll tokens. These are typically the easiest and quickest way to get a significant boost, sometimes granting hundreds of tokens.
*   **Daily Rewards:** Don't forget your daily login bonuses. On specific days, usually day six, you can claim 25 trait reroll tokens. It's a small but consistent source that requires no effort beyond logging in.
*   **Quests (Daily & Weekly):** Engage with your daily and weekly quests. While they might not offer a huge quantity, completing these tasks will often reward you with a handful of trait reroll tokens, along with other valuable resources.
*   **Level Rewards:** As your player level increases, you'll periodically receive trait reroll tokens. Typically, every five levels grants you five tokens. While this rate is relatively low and could benefit from an increase, it's a passive reward you'll accumulate naturally as you play.
*   **Normal Challenges:** Participating in normal challenges offers one trait reroll token per completion. For new players, these might be a good starting point to get a few tokens, but the reward-to-effort ratio is quite low compared to other methods.
*   **Daily Challenges:** Stepping up from normal challenges, daily challenges offer a more respectable eight trait reroll tokens, alongside gems and stars for unit evolution. These can be more challenging for new players but are a good target for mid-game players looking for a consistent daily source.

## Battle Pass and Raid Rewards

## The Most Efficient Farming Methods for Trait Rerolls

When it comes to truly maximizing your trait reroll token acquisition in *Anime Story 2*, two primary methods stand out: the Slime Raid and the Tower Event. These are generally considered the "best" methods for dedicated farming.

### The Slime Raid: A Challenging Grind

The Slime Raid is a difficult encounter that, upon completion, rewards you with slime tokens and a single trait reroll token. While one token might seem negligible, the true value lies in the slime tokens. These tokens can be used at the banner to summon various items, including stat chips, mythic orbs, and potentially more trait reroll tokens or secret orbs.

*   **Strategy for Slime Raid:** The Slime Raid is notoriously tough. Many players find it challenging, and success often relies on having a specific team composition. The *Anime Story 2* Discord server's "tier list" channel is an invaluable resource, often featuring recommended beginner teams that can clear the Slime Raid. Units like Broly and Greed are frequently cited as essential. Even with a strong team, RNG can play a role, so don't be discouraged by occasional losses. The key is persistence and optimizing your team based on community advice.

### The Tower Event: The Ultimate Trait Reroll Source

Currently, the Tower Event is widely regarded as the absolute best method for consistently farming a large number of trait reroll tokens. This event offers insane rewards for progressing through its difficult stages.

*   **Tower Event Mechanics:**
    *   **Floor Rewards:** Every fifth floor you clear in the Tower Event grants you five trait reroll tokens. This alone provides a steady stream as you ascend.
    *   **Loop Rewards:** The tower loops every 250 floors. Upon resetting the tower after reaching floor 250 (or any multiple of 250), you receive an extra 100 trait reroll tokens. This means for every 250 floors you complete, you get a significant bonus on top of the individual floor rewards.
    *   **Difficulty Scaling:** The tower's difficulty increases significantly near the end of each 250-floor loop, requiring increasingly powerful units and strategic play. However, as your units grow stronger, you'll find it easier to push further.
    *   **Reset Schedule:** The Tower Event typically resets with major game updates (e.g., every 2.5 updates). This allows players to re-engage with the challenge and earn rewards again.

The Tower Event's combination of consistent per-floor rewards and massive loop bonuses makes it the most efficient way to acquire a large quantity of trait reroll tokens for mid to end-game players. It incentivizes continuous progression and unit improvement, directly rewarding your efforts with the resources needed to further optimize your roster.

## Strategic Spending and Bundles

While free-to-play methods are plentiful, there are also options for players willing to spend Robux.

*   **Bundles:** The in-game store often features bundles that offer trait reroll tokens alongside other boosts. For example, Bundle Six might offer 250 trait reroll tokens for 250 Robux. These can be a quick way to get a significant number of tokens if you're in urgent need. However, always evaluate the overall value. Some bundles might include units that require further evolution (like an unevolved Shiny Greed), which might not be immediately useful if you lack the necessary evolution materials (e.g., cups from World 7 or 8). It's generally advised that progression in *Anime Story 2* is achievable without purchasing bundles, but they offer a shortcut for those who desire it.
